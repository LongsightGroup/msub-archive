Types of Group Sites

Group sites in the CLE are subdivided into four major types:

•      Course sites (designed for course management)

•      Project sites (designed for online collaboration)

•      Portfolio sites (designed for creating, viewing, and evaluating ePortfolios)

•      Administration sites (designed for administering the CLE at your institution)

The first three types of sites are designed for end users, including instructors, students, project organizers, and participants. Administration sites are designed for site administrators only. This guide covers course, project, and portfolio sites – the sites for end users. It does not cover CLE administration sites.

Note: For information about administration sites, see The rSmart Sakai CLE Administrator Guide.
