Sections

Accessing the Sections Home Page

Note: Your view of screens in this tool will vary slightly, depending on whether you are an instructor or a teaching assistant and how your school has chosen to configure the CLE and implement a student information system (SIS) integration. If your courses and sections are automatically created by the SIS, you may view, but not edit, sections in your course.

The Sections tool is available only in course sites. To access the Sections tool, click Sections in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Sections home page.

This page lists the sections of your course, along with information such as the day and time it meets, the names of assigned TAs, the meeting location for the course, and size information.
