Evaluation System

For Evaluators: Taking an Evaluation

To take an evaluation, follow these steps:

1.  Access the evaluation in any of these ways:

•      To access it from the notification email you received, either click the evaluation link provided or proceed as directed in the Alternate Instructions section at the bottom of the email.

•      Log into the CLE. In My Workspace, access the Evaluation System tool by selecting it from the tools menu on the left side of the screen. Click the linked group or site name associated with the evaluation you want to take.

•      If you have been given the direct link or the permalink for the evaluation, paste this URL into the Address bar of your browser. Depending on the settings for the evaluation, you may need to log into the CLE before viewing the evaluation.

The CLE displays the evaluation.

The layout, number, and display of questions and answer options and labels may vary greatly from one evaluation to another.

2.  If the evaluation includes a Switch Group dropdown list under the name of the evaluation, you belong to two groups of evaluators and may take this evaluation as a member of either group. To change groups, select from the dropdown list and click the Switch button.

3.  Following instructions (if any), click to select an answer to each question. (Depending on how the evaluation was set up, you may not be allowed to skip questions.)

4.  Click Submit Evaluation. The CLE submits the evaluation and changes your status for this evaluation to completed.
