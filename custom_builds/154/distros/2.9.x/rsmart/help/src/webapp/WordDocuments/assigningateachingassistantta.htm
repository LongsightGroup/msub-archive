Assigning a Teaching Assistant (TA)

After you have created sections, you may add TAs to them.

Note: Before assigning TAs to a section, you must assign the role of TA to the appropriate site participants. To assign the TA role to one or more participants, see Adding Participants.

To assign TAs to a section, access the Sections tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Sections menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the section to which you want to assign TAs, and click the Assign TAs link for it.

The CLE displays the Assign Teaching Assistants screen.

3.  Do either of the following:

•      To add all TAs in the box on the left to this section, click the two arrows for Move All to the right. The CLE moves the names of all these TAs to the box on the right.

•      To select some but not all TAs from the list on the left, hold the CTRL or Apple key down as you select those you wish to add; then click the single right arrow. The CLE moves the names of these TAs to the box on the right.

4.  To assign these students to this section, click Assign TAs. The CLE displays the Sections home page, along with this confirmation message:

Or, to return to the home page without assigning students, click Cancel.
