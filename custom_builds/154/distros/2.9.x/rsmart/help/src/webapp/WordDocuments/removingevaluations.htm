Evaluation System

Removing Evaluations

Note: You cannot remove an evaluation that has responses.

To remove an evaluation, access the Evaluation System tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you are not on the Evaluation System home page, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  In the menu bar at the top of the screen, click My Evaluations. The CLE displays the My Evaluations screen.

3.  Find the evaluation you want and click the Remove link for it. The CLE displays the Remove confirmation screen.

4.  To proceed, click Remove Evaluation. The CLE displays the My Evaluations screen. (Or, to return to that screen without removing the evaluation, click Cancel.)
