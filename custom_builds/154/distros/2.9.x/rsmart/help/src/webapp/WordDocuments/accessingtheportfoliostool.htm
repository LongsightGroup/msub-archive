Portfolios

Accessing the Portfolios Tool

To access the tool, click Portfolios in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Portfolios home page.

This page displays lists of all portfolios on the site that you have permission to view. You have several options for viewing the list.

•      To see only your own portfolios, click the My Portfolios tab.

•      To see Portfolios that other users are sharing with you, click the Portfolios Being Shared with Me tab.

•      To see portfolios that are publicly accessible, click the Public Portfolios tab.

Within each list, you have these additional options.

•      To see only hidden portfolios click the Hidden link.

•      To see only those portfolios that are not hidden, click the Not Hidden link.

•      To see both groups of portfolios, click the All link.

For each portfolio listed, you can see the owner’s name, when it was last updated, whether it has been reviewed, whether it is actively being shared with others (inactive portfolios are listed in gray type and the Active column is empty), whether it is publicly accessible, whether you have specified users with whom to share it, and how many comments have been made on it.  An Actions dropdown list provides quick access to functions you may want to perform on the portfolio.
