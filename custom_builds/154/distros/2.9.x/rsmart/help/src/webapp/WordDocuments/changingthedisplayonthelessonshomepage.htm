Changing the Display on the Lessons Home Page

You can choose whether the default display on the Lessons home page will include modules and their sections (expanded view) or modules alone (collapsed view). To change this setting, access the Lessons tool and follow these steps:

1.  Click the Preferences link near the top of the screen. The CLE displays the User Preference Options screen.

2.  Click the button for your preferred display.

3.  Click Set.
