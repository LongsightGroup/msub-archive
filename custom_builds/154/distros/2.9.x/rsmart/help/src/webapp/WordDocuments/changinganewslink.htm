Changing a News Link

You may change the name of a news link and the URL to which it points. To make changes, access the appropriate News tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the News menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Options on the News menu bar. The CLE displays this screen:

3.  Change the entry in the Tool Title, Page Title and/or URL boxes as needed. Entries in these fields are required.

4.  To proceed, click Update Options. You may also exit without applying the changes by clicking Cancel.
