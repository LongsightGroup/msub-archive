Adding Special Access Settings

You may provide select students with “special access” display and due date settings for a forum. To do so, access the Discussion Forums tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Discussion Forums home page, click Manage.

2.  In the Forums listing screen, click on the “special access” icon () for the appropriate forum. The CLE displays the Special Access screen:

 

     Click the Add button. The CLE displays a list of site participants, as well as       visibility and due date settings:

3.  Select the appropriate user(s) and specify the appropriate Invisible Until, Due Date/Time, and Lock on Due settings. You may specify settings for single or multiple users.

4.  Click update. The CLE displays the users and their settings:

 

To add special access settings for more users, click the Add button. To remove special access settings, click on the checkbox for the appropriate user(s) and click Delete Selected.

Tip: If you have set up grading by forum and are entering scores for a forum in the grading screen, you may provide a particular student with special access settings for the forum by clicking on the special access icon for that student. For more information, see Grading by Category, Forum, or Topic.
