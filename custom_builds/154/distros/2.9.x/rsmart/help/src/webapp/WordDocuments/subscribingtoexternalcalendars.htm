Subscribing to External Calendars

Note: The subscription function works with any online calendar data that is in iCalendar (iCal) format.

To subscribe to one or more external online calendars (that is, calendars that are external to the CLE) in order to display data, access the Calendar tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you are not on the Calendar home page, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Subscriptions (import) on the menu bar. The CLE displays the Calendar Subscriptions screen.

3.  Enter a descriptive name in the Calendar Name field.

4.  Enter the URL for the calendar whose data you want to display.

5.  Click Subscribe. The CLE displays the calendar in the bottom half of the screen.

6.  To subscribe to additional calendars, repeat steps 3-5.

7.  To remove a calendar to which you are currently subscribed, clear the appropriate box in the Subscribed? column.

8.  After completing your work, click Save. The CLE displays the Calendar home page. Or, to return to that page without saving your changes, click Cancel.
