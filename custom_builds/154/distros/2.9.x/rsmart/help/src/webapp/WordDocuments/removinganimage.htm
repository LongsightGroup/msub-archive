Removing an Image

To remove an image in the Profile tool, access the tool and follow these steps:

1.  Access your Pictures gallery. You may do so in one of two ways:

•      Click on Pictures in the tool menu bar.

•      If you have opted to display your images on your profile, click on the View all pictures link in your profile’s “My Pictures” collection.

     In either case, the CLE will display your Pictures gallery, with thumbnails of all
     the images you have uploaded.

2.  Find the image you want to remove and click on its thumbnail. The CLE displays a larger view of the image, with two buttons:

3.  Click on the Remove picture button. The CLE will display the following for the image:

 

4.  If you do not want to remove the image, click the Cancel button. Otherwise, click on the Confirm removal button.

The image will no longer be displayed in your Pictures gallery or in the “My Pictures” collection on your profile.

Note: If you have opted to set an image in your Pictures gallery as your profile image, as instructed in Setting an Image as Your Profile Image, removing that image from your Pictures gallery will NOT remove it as your profile image. To change an image profile, proceed as instructed in Editing Your Profile.
