Adding Images

To upload images to your Pictures gallery in the Profile tool, access the tool and follow these steps:

1.  Access the Pictures gallery. You may do so in one of two ways:

•      Click on Pictures in the tool menu bar.

•      If you have opted to display your images on your profile, click on the View all pictures link in your profile’s “My Pictures” collection.

     In either case, the CLE displays your Pictures gallery:

2.  In the “Add picture” section, click the Browse button and select the appropriate image from your computer. You may continue to select images, for a maximum of 10 images. Keep in mind, however, that the total upload size for all images selected cannot exceed 20 MB.

The CLE will then display a list of your selected files:

3.  If you do not want to upload a file in the list, click the Delete button for the appropriate file. Otherwise, click the Upload chosen files button.

Once the upload is complete, the image(s) will be displayed in your Pictures gallery.
