Resume Taking a Saved Assessment

To resume taking an assessment you have saved, access the Tests & Quizzes tool and follow these steps:

1.  Find the appropriate assessment in the Take an Assessment section and click its title. The CLE displays the Begin Assessment screen.

2.  Click Begin Assessment. The CLE goes to the point at which you exited earlier and displays the last question you accessed in the assessment.

3.  Resume your work on the assessment.
