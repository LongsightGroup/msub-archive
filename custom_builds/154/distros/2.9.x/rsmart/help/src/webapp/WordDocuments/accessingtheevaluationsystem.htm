Evaluation System

Accessing the Evaluation System

By default, the Evaluation System tool is available in each user’s My Workspace. To access the Evaluation System tool, click Evaluation System in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Evaluation System home page.

The layout of the home page (which is also known as the Evaluation Dashboard) varies, depending on how much information your institution has chosen to display. At a minimum, you will see two lists. On the left is a list of evaluations to which you have been assigned as an evaluator. For each evaluation in this section, you see:

•      Evaluation title.

•      Name of the course or evaluator group with which the evaluation is associated. If the name is displayed as a link, this evaluation is available for you to take now.

•      Status of this evaluation (pending=ready for you to take;  completed=you have already taken it).

•      Start and end dates for this evaluation.

On the right is a list of evaluations that are underway, not yet available to evaluators, or recently closed. This list is grouped in two sections:

All evaluations in which you may be evaluated—including evaluations that have been created, and/or are being administered by, other users in the CLE.

Evaluations you have created, and/or are administering

In each list, you see:

•      Evaluation title.

•      Status of this evaluation (e.g., active, in queue, viewable).

•      Key dates that vary depending on the evaluation’s status.

For the list of evaluations in which you may be evaluated, you will also see the number of evaluators who have responded to each evaluation and the total number of evaluators assigned.

Note: If other view options have been turned on at your institution, you may also see lists of groups you may evaluate and groups you may be evaluated by.

In addition to the usual tool menu at the top of the screen, embedded links allow you to quickly access evaluations you need to take, previews of evaluations you are creating or administering, and reports for your closed evaluations.

To return to this screen from another screen in the tool, either click Evaluations Dashboard in the menu bar at the top of the screen or click the blue Reset button.
