Tools in Course Sites

As in all sites, a course site offers various tools that help you accomplish your tasks. These tools are listed in a tools menu that runs down the left side of the screen.

Course tools are grouped together into three categories: Communication, Course Tools, and Site Management. Only the Home and Help tools are listed in the tools menu without belonging to a category.

The tools menu on your CLE course site contains only the tools you have permission to use. If you do not have permission to use any tools in a particular category, the category name is not listed in the menu.

The following tables summarize the tools in each category that are typically available to users in at least one role in a course site.

 

Tool

Purpose

Announcements

Allows you to post announcements relevant to this project.

Calendar

Allows you to maintain a calendar for a course.

Discussion Forums

Allows you to create, moderate, participate, and manage discussion groups and topics within your course and send and receive private messages.

Messages

Allows you to send and receive private messages.

Forums

Allows you to create, moderate, manage and grade participation in discussion groups and topics within your course.

Mailtool

Allows you to send email to anyone.

Email Archive

Allows you to view a list of messages that have been sent to your course site.

Chat Room

Allows you engage in real-time, unstructured conversations with your students.

Live Virtual Classroom

If your institution has a license for Elluminate Live, allows you access this virtual classroom environment.

Blogs

Allows you to create and maintain a blog (that is, a running commentary on topics of your choice) and display comments by others.

Polls

Allows students to express their opinions anonymously by voting on a question.

Syllabus

Allows you to post an outline of the units in your course.

Lessons

Allows you to create, publish, and manage online learning sequences for your course.

Resources

Allows you to post, store, and organize material related to teaching your course.

Assignments

Allows you to post assignments online; allows students to submit work on assignments and view grades and feedback they have received.

Tests & Quizzes

Allows you to create and administer online assessments; allows students to take assessments online and view grades and feedback they've received.

Drop Box

Allows students to share documents with you in private, personal folders.

Gradebook

Allows you to enter grades on line and control display of these grades to students.

Gradebook2

Allows you to enter grades on line and control display of these grades to students (alternative to Gradebook tool).

Feedback

Allows you to provide feedback to students on their assignments.

Podcasts

Allows you to store podcasts on your site.

Wiki

Allows students to communicate or work collaboratively on written categories.

News

Allows you to display a dynamic syndicated news source to your students.

Web Content

Allows you to display the contents of an external web site to your students.

Site Editor

Allows you to create, edit, and manage the course site.

Sections

Allows you to create and manage sections of the course.

Site Statistics

Allows you to view statistics on site usage.

Roster

Allows students to view a list of students in a class. Any information a student has chosen to make public will appear here.

Search

Allows you to search for items in the site that contain words or phrases you specify.

Home

Displays the site home page.

Help

Displays the online help system for the CLE.
