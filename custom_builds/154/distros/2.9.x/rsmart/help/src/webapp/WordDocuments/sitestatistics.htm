Site Statistics

The Site Statistics tool allows you to display user statistics for your site, organized in both tables and graphs. You may view:

•      Number of visits to your site, as well as which users visited.

•      Activities performed by users on your site, and which tools they used to perform them.

•      Resource items that users created/accessed on your site.

You may group information in several different ways, such as by date, by user, by tool, and by resource. You may also apply different filters to your information, such as a specific time period, user role, tool used, or file action taken.   

In addition to viewing information in the CLE, you may also print reports and export them as XLS, CSV, or PDF files. You may select reports from the Overview screen or generate your own customized reports, which you may save in the CLE on the tool's Reports screen. This tool is normally available only in course and project sites.
