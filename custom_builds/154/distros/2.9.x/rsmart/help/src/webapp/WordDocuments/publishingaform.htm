Publishing a Form

In order to make a form available to site participants, you must publish it.

Warning:Make certain that all aspects of a form are correct before you publish it. After publication, you can change only the form name and the instructions for completing the form. You cannot change the fields and field names in the form, and you cannot delete the form. Therefore, you must make sure that all aspects of the form itself are exactly as you want them before publishing the form.

 

Note: To review a form prior to publishing it, follow the instructions in Viewing a Form.

 

Tip: If you have published a form that you no longer want site participants to use, rename it in a way that alerts users to the fact that it is not to be used. For example, you might rename the form Certifications as Certifications OLD or Certifications Do Not Use.

To publish a form, access the Forms tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Forms home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the form you want to publish.

3.  Click the Publish link for it. The CLE displays this confirmation screen:

4.  To publish the form, click Yes. The CLE displays the Forms home page. The Site State for this form has been changed to “published.” Or, to return to the home page without publishing the form, click No.
