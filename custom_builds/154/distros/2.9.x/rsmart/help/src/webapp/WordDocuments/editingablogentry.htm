Editing a Blog Entry

To edit a blog entry, follow these steps:

1.  If the home page is not displayed, click the Reset button or click All the blogs in the Blogs menu bar at the top of the page.

2.  Click the My blog link.

3.  For the appropriate blog entry, click the Edit entry link. The CLE displays the Edit a blog entry page.

4.  Make all appropriate changes to the entry’s content and/or settings, as described in steps 2-5 in Adding a Blog Entry.
