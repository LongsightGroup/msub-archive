Changing Your Password and Account Details

To change your password, access the Account tool and follow these steps:

1.  Click Modify Details. The CLE displays boxes in which you can update specific information about yourself.

2.  Change information as necessary. To change your password, enter the new password in both the Create New Password and Verify New Password boxes.

3.  To save your changes, click Update Details. The CLE displays the Account home page again. To return to the home page without saving your changes, click Cancel Changes.
