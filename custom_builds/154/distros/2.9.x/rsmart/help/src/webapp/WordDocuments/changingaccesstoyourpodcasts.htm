Changing Access to Your Podcasts

This tool allows you to make your podcasts available to site members only or to the public. The setting you choose applies to all podcasts on the site. You cannot make some but not all podcasts publicly available.

To view and change the setting for access to podcasts, access the Podcasts tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Podcasts home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Options in the menu bar near the top of the screen. The CLE displays the Options screen.

3.  Click a radio button to select the appropriate option.

4.  To save your work and return to the Podcasts home page, click the Save Changes button. Or, to exit without saving your changes, click Cancel.

If you have changed access, the CLE displays an alert message on the home page.
