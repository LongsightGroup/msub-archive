Setting Preferences in the Profile Tool

To set preferences in the Profile tool, click on Preferences in the menu bar. The CLE displays the following screen:

You may set preferences for email notifications, Twitter integration, and widget settings (the display of your “kudos” rating and pictures).

Email Notifications

By default, the CLE will send you an email notification when another user does any of the following:

•      Adds you as a connection (sends a connection request)

•      Confirms your connection request

•      Sends you a new message

•      Replies to a message you have sent

Keep in mind that you may only send messages to, or receive messages from, your profile connections

If you do not want to receive these notifications, click the appropriate Off radio buttons. Then, click the Save settings button.

Twitter Integration

Note: To post updates in Twitter from the Profile tool, you must have a Twitter account.

By default, the CLE will NOT send your status updates to your Twitter account. If you want your Profile status updates to appear on your Twitter home page, follow these steps:

1.  Click on Link Twitter account. You will be taken to Twitter in a separate browser window/tab. If necessary, sign in. You will then be asked to allow the Profile tool access.

2.  Click the Allow button. Twitter will display a message that you have successfully granted access, along with a PIN you must enter in the CLE.

3.  Enter, or copy and paste, this PIN into the blank field on the Profile Preferences page.

4.  Click the Link button. The CLE will display "Twitter account linked," along with your Twitter user name:


If you no longer want your Twitter account linked, click on the red "X" icon next to your Twitter user name.

Widget Settings

The widget settings allow you to control the display of your “kudos” rating and pictures on your profile.

•      Click on the checkbox for Show my kudos rating to display the rating. This daily rating for your profile is based on various factors, such as the amount of information you have provided, whether you display a profile image, and the number of status updates you have posted.      

•      Click on Show my pictures to display images you have uploaded to your Pictures gallery. The images will be displayed on your profile in a “My Pictures” collection, directly below your profile connections.
