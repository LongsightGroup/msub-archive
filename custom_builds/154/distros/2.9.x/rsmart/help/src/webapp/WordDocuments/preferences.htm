Preferences

The Preferences tool allows you to manage your Quicklinks, change email notification options, specify your time zone and language preferences, and set your privacy status (site visibility). The settings you choose here apply to all of your sites, with some exceptions. You may, for example, set Matrices and Wizards notification preferences, as well as your privacy status, for individual sites. This tool is normally available only in My Workspace.

To access the Preferences tool, click Preferences in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Preferences home page.

Your home page is also known as the Customize Tabs screen. It contains two lists for the sites you belong to—one for active sites (sites that are listed when you click the My Sites tab) and the other for hidden sites (sites that are not listed in the My Sites lists). The first few sites in the My Active Sites list are displayed as Quicklinks at the top of the screen. All other active sites are displayed when you click the My Sites tab.
