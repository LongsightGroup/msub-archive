Setting Your Notification Preferences

You may receive a notification email when feedback or an evaluation has been provided for your matrix. To specify your notification preference, follow these steps:

1.  If the Matrices home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  In the tool menu bar, click My Preferences. The CLE displays  the following window:

3.  Select your preference.

•      If you want to receive all email notifications, select Send me each notification separately.

•      If you want to receive one notification summary each day, select Send me one email per day summarizing all notifications.

•      If you do not want to receive any email notifications, select Do not send me notifications.

4.  To update your changes for the site you're in, click Update Matrices Preferences. To update your changes for all sites that include the Matrices tool, select Update Matrices Preferences for All Sites. If you do not want to save your changes, click Cancel Changes.

Tip: You may also set your notification preferences using the Preferences tool, as instructed in Setting Notification Preferences.
