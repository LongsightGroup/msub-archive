Providing Formative Feedback on a Wizard

If you have appropriate permissions to act as a reviewer, you may review and provide formative feedback on the work of any site participant in any wizard in the site at any time. Your feedback may be specific to a particular evidence item a participant has attached to a page or it may take the form of general feedback on the page. You may also add feedback on the wizard as a whole.

When you review work done in wizards, each participant's work is displayed in read-only mode.

To provide feedback, you fill out a form that is linked to the wizard as a whole or to a particular page or evidence item and give your completed form a unique title. This completed form is then available to the site participant. You may fill out the same form any number of times (with a different title each time) to provide feedback on changes the participant may have made. You may review and provide feedback at any time up until the participant submits the wizard or the wizard page for summative evaluation.

To review and provide feedback on a wizard, access the Wizards tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Wizards home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  In the list of wizards, click the name of the wizard you want to review. The CLE displays the wizard.

3.  Use the Select user dropdown list to select the name of the person whose work in the wizard you want to review. If your site has groups, the CLE will also display a Select group dropdown menu for filtering the users you select.

 

The CLE displays the wizard for that user.

4.  To add feedback on the wizard as a whole, scroll down to the bottom of the screen, expand the Feedback section header if necessary, and click the Add Feedback link. Then proceed to step 8.

5.  Click the name of a page you want to review. The CLE displays the page.

6.  Choose either of these options:

•      Enter feedback for a specific item by clicking the Add Feedback link for it.

•      Add feedback on the page in general by clicking Add Feedback in the General Feedback section at the bottom of the screen.

 

The CLE displays a screen containing a feedback form. It may look something like this:

7.  Enter information in the fields as appropriate.

Warning: The Access section of the screen allows you to make your feedback public to people other than the participant whose work you are reviewing. As a general rule, we advise against making feedback public in any way. To keep your feedback between you and the participant, do not click either radio button in this section.

8.  To add your feedback, click Save Changes. The CLE displays the wizard page again. The title of your feedback is shown in the Feedback section of the screen.

9.  Click Back to the Wizard.

10. To add more feedback, repeat steps 6 - 10.

11. After providing feedback on all appropriate pages and or evidence items, click Return to Wizards to return to the Wizards home page.
