My Workspace Information

On your My Workspace home page, My Workspace Information contains general information about your My Workspace site. This text was created by your system administrator. You cannot change the text, but you may change its title, and you may change the height of the frame in which it is displayed. To do so, follow these steps:

1.  Click Options in the menu bar near the top of My Workspace Information.

 

The CLE displays the Customize My Workspace Info Display.

2.  Change the entry in the Title box, if desired. An entry in this box is required.

3.  Use the pulldown list to change the Frame Height, if desired.

4.  To save your changes, click Update Options. Or, to close the display without saving your changes, click Cancel.
