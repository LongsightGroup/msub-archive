Setting Notification Preferences

You may set email notification preferences for the following tools:

•      Announcements

•      Resources and Drop Box

•      Syllabus

•      Email Archive

•      Matrices

•      Wizards

Note: With the Preferences tool, you may choose not to receive low-priority notifications. You will still receive all high-priority notifications, however, regardless of the preferences you select in this tool.

To set notification preferences, access the Preferences tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Preferences menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Notifications in the menu bar. The CLE displays the Notifications screen:

3.  Make any necessary changes by selecting the buttons corresponding to your desired preferences.

4.  To save your changes, click Update Preferences. The CLE displays the Preferences home page again, with a message that your changes have been updated successfully. Or, to return to the home page without saving your changes, click Cancel Changes.
