Adding Students to a Section

Note: You may not add students to a full section.

After you have created sections, you may add students to them. To do so, access the Sections tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Sections menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the section to which you want to assign students, and click the Assign Students link for it.

The CLE displays the Assign Students screen.

3.  To specify the section you want to draw students from, select its name from the pulldown list. Or select Unassigned Students. In the box on the left side of the screen, the CLE displays a list of students.

4.  Do either of the following:

•      To add all students in the box on the left to this section, click the two arrows for Move All to the right. The CLE moves the names of all these students to the box on the right.

•      To select some but not all students from the list on the left, hold the CTRL or Apple key down as you select those you wish to add; then click the single right arrow. The CLE moves the names of these students to the box on the right.

5.  To assign these students to this section, click Assign students. The CLE displays the Sections home page, along with this confirmation message:

Or, to return to the home page without assigning students, click Cancel.
