Live Virtual Classroom

If your institution uses Elluminate Live, the Live Virtual Classroom tool gives you quick access to Elluminate’s virtual classroom environment within the CLE. The tool also enables you to perform these activities:

•      Schedule one-time and re-occurring Elluminate sessions.

•      Edit and delete scheduled Elluminate sessions and archived recordings.

•      Join active Elluminate sessions.

•      Watch recordings of past sessions.

•      Filter and sort lists of your sessions and recordings.
