Overriding Matrices and Wizards Preferences

You may override Matrices and Wizards preferences for specific portfolio sites to which you belong. To do so, access the Preferences tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Preferences menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Notifications in the menu bar. The CLE displays the Notifications screen.

3.  In the Matrices or Wizards notification section, click Add a site.

The CLE displays a screen similar to the following:

4.  Click on the checkbox for the appropriate site(s), and then click Add Site(s). On the Notifications screen, the CLE displays a separate notification dropdown menu for each selected site:

5.  Select the appropriate notification option from the dropdown menu(s).

6.  To save your changes, click Update Preferences. The CLE displays the Preferences home page again, with a message that your changes have been updated successfully. Or, to return to the home page without saving your changes, click Cancel Changes. To remove the notification override for a site, click the “remove” icon () next to the dropdown menu for that site.

Tip: You may also set notification preferences in the Matrices and Wizards tools, as instructed in Setting Notification Preferences for the Matrices Tool and Setting Notification Preferences for the Wizards Tool.
