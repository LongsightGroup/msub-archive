Deleting a Discussion Topic

Warning: When you delete a topic, you also delete all messages in it.

To delete a topic, access the Forums tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Forums home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the appropriate topic and click the Topic Settings link for it. The CLE displays the Topic Settings screen.

3.  To delete this topic, click the Delete button near the bottom of the screen. The CLE displays this message:

4.  To proceed to delete the topic and its contents, click the Delete button near the bottom of the screen again. Or, to return to the Forums home page without deleting it, click Cancel.
