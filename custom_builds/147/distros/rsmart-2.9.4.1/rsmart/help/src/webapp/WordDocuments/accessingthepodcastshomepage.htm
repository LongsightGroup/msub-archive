Podcasts

Accessing the Podcasts Home Page

To access this tool, click Podcasts in the tools menu on the left side of your screen. The CLE displays the Podcasts home page.

 

This screen lists the Podcasts stored in the Podcasts tool for this site, with the most recently available listed first. For each podcast in the list, the display includes the following:

•      Date and time the podcast has been or will be made available to participants (by default, site participants will not see a podcast in the list until this date and time)

•      Title of the podcast

•      Links for downloading, editing, and deleting the podcast; by default, site participants will see only the Download link

•      File size and type (after the Download link)

•      Name of the person who created or last changed the podcast, along with the date and time
