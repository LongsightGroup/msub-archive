Viewing and Managing Your Blog Posts

To access all of your blog posts, click View my blog in the tool menu bar. The CLE displays your profile information, as well as a list of all the blog posts you’ve created:

The list will include both published posts and drafts, and it will be in reverse chronological order, with the most recent post displayed first. The content of each post will be displayed if you have not selected the Show title only option, and you can click on a post title to view only that post. You can also edit or delete a post.

Note: The information displayed for your profile in the Clog tool will depend on the content you provided in the Profile tool, as well as the privacy settings you selected in that tool.
