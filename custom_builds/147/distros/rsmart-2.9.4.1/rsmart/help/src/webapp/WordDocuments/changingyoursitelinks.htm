Changing Your Site Links

In the CLE, you can display links to sites along the top of the screen. You may change the site links you want displayed, as well as their order from left to right.

To change your site links, follow these steps:

1.  Access the Customize Tabs page in the Preferences tool. You can do so in one of the following ways: 

•      In My Workspace, select the Preferences tool. Then, click on Customize Tabs in the menu bar.

•      Depending on how your CLE instance is configured, you can also access the Customize Tabs page by clicking Edit to the right of your site links, or by clicking Preferences in the sites drawer (see Navigating to a Site) or the user menu (see Navigating with the User Menu).

The sites for which you can display site links will be listed in the My Active Sites box on the left.

Note: Site links cannot be displayed for hidden sites. To make a hidden site active, proceed as instructed in Activating Hidden Sites.

2.  To change the number of site links displayed, select a different number from the Tabs displayed dropdown menu. 

3.  The order of sites in the My Active Sites box determines the order of site links, from left to right. Moving a site down the list, for example, results in its site link being displayed farther to the right in the row(s) of links at the top of the screen. To change the order of sites:

a.  Click the site you want to move up or down the list.

b.  Click the up () or down () arrow as needed to move the site to the desired position.

4.  To save your changes, click Update Preferences.
