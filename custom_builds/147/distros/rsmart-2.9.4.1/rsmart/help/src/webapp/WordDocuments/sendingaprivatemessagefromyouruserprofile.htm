Sending a Private Message from Your User Profile

From the user profile, you can quickly contact a site participant via private message or instant messaging.

•      To send a private message, click the PM icon and follow the instructions in Sending a Private Message. Begin with step 3.

•      To send an instant message, click the ICQ icon or use the number displayed to send an instant message via another service.

•      To send an email, click the email icon and proceed as usual.
