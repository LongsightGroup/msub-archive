Searching for Content

To search for content in the wiki, access the Wiki tool and follow these steps:

1.  Enter one or more search terms in the Search box.

Note: The Wiki tool recognizes the 'and' operator as 'and/or'. For example, searching for 'children and dahlias' will find all pages that contain the word 'children' and the word 'dahlias' and all pages that contain either 'children' or 'dahlias.’

2.  Press ENTER. The CLE displays a list of all pages in the wiki that contain the search term(s) you entered. The CLE also adds this search to the breadcrumb trail so you can return to your results page at any time.

Note: The Wiki tool searches only pages that are in the Wiki. It does not search pages in other parts of the CLE (such as, Resources and Syllabus). Neither does it search attachments to the wiki.
