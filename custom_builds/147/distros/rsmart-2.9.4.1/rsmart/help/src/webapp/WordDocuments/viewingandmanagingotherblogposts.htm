Viewing and Managing Other Blog Posts

To access the blog posts of all site participants, click on View authors in the menu bar. The CLE displays a list of all blog authors:

For each site participant, you’ll see:

•      total number of published blog posts

•      date/time of the most recent post

•      total number of comments for blog posts

You can sort this list by author name, number of posts, date of last post, or number of comments.

To view all blog posts for a particular user, click on that user’s name. You can also subscribe to an RSS feed for the posts of a user by clicking on the appropriate RSS icon ().

Tip: You can view all blog posts in the comprehensive display of posts on the home page, if you have not selected the Show title only option. Or, you can click on a post title to display only that post.
