Overview of fMath Menus

Below is a summary of menu functions in the fMath editor. Functions are listed in the order they are displayed in the menu, from left to right (or top to bottom, if the menu is displayed vertically on the side of the editor).

To move a menu, click on the icon () at the end of the menu and drag it to any location at the top or side of the editor.
