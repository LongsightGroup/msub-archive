Removing Participants

To remove a participant from your site, access the Site Editor Home page and follow these steps:

1.  Find the participant's name in the list and check the box to the right of the “Status” menu for that name.
 

2.  Click Update Participants. The CLE displays the changed list with a reduced number of users. The participant name you removed is no longer displayed.

Tip: To select the “Remove” box for all site participants, check the box for Select/unselect all participants.
