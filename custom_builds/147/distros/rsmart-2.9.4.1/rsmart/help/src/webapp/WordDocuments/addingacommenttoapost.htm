Adding a Comment to a Post

To comment on a blog post, follow these steps:

•      Click on the Comment on this post icon () for the appropriate post.

•      In the rich text editor that is displayed, enter your comment. Use the tool menu in the editor to format text and insert content or links, as appropriate.

•      Click Save.

You will then see the comment displayed within the blog post. Keep in mind that comments for a post are listed in chronological order, with the most recent comment displayed last.
