Providing Formative Feedback on a Matrix

If you are a reviewer, you may review the cells of a site participant's matrix at any time and offer formative feedback on the work you find there. You may also return to a cell you have reviewed before, review any further work that has been done there, and provide additional feedback.

To review and provide feedback on a matrix, access the Matrices tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Matrices home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the matrix you want to review and click its name. The CLE displays a screen like this one:

The screen displays the matrix. In the upper left corner of the screen, a Select user dropdown list is displayed. If your site has groups, the CLE will also display a Select group dropdown menu for filtering the users you select.

3.  Use the dropdown list to select the name of the person whose matrix you want.

The CLE displays a read-only copy of the matrix for the user you specified.

Icons in a cell indicate that the user has attached material to it. The background color of a cell indicates its status.

4.  To review the contents of a cell, click on it. The CLE displays the cell. The display might look something like this:

5.  To review an attached item, click its name.

6.  To enter feedback on this item:

a.  Click the Add Feedback link for it. The CLE displays the appropriate feedback form. It may look something like this:

b.  Enter comments in the Comments box, and then add a title for your feedback (required) and, if desired, a description.

c.  To add your feedback, click Save Changes. The CLE displays the contents of the cell again. The title of your feedback is shown in the Evidence Items section of the screen. Or to return to that screen without saving your feedback, click Cancel.

7.  To enter feedback on the cell as a whole:

a.  In the General Feedback section, click the Add Feedback link. The CLE displays the appropriate feedback form.  

b.  Enter your feedback and save your work as explained for entering feedback for an attached item.

8.  To return to the matrix display, click Back to the Matrix.
