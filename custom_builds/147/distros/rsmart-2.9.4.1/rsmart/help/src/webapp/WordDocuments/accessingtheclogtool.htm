Accessing the Clog Tool

When you first select the Clog tool, the CLE displays the home page. If you’re an instructor, the home page might look similar to this:

On the home page, the CLE displays your posts and the posts of all other site participants.

•      The posts are displayed in reverse chronological order, with the most recent post listed first.

•      Comments for a post are displayed within that post. They are displayed in chronological order, with the most recent post listed last.

•      If you want only the titles of posts to be displayed, and not the content as well, check the Show title only box.

•      To search for content in posts, enter the appropriate text in the Search field.

•      To subscribe to an RSS feed for posts by all site participants, click on the RSS icon ().

From the menu bar on the home page, you may:

•      View blog posts and profile information for all users by clicking View authors.

•      View and edit your own blog posts by clicking View my blog.

•      Create new blog posts, by clicking Create new post.

•      Edit tool permissions for site members, by clicking Permissions.

•      Access deleted posts, by clicking Recycle Bin.

Keep in mind that students will typically not have access to tool permissions or the recycle bin.
