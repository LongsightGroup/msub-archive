Forms

Accessing the Forms Tool

To access the Forms tool, click Forms in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Forms home page.

The home page contains a list of forms that have been created locally for your site. The forms are ordered alphabetically. For each form, you can see the site on which the form is published, when it was last changed, and its state (published, unpublished, or waiting for approval) in this site and across the entire system.

If the list of forms is longer than one page, use the buttons under the Forms menu bar to display other pages in the list.

•      To display the first page in the list of forms, click First.

•      To display the previous page in the list of forms, click Previous.

•      To display the next page in the list of forms, click Next.

•      To display the last page in the list of forms, click Last.
