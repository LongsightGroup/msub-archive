Twitter Integration

Note: To post updates in Twitter from the Profile tool, you must have a Twitter account.

By default, the CLE will NOT send your status updates to your Twitter account. If you want your Profile status updates to appear on your Twitter home page, follow these steps:

1.  Click on Preferences in the Profile tool menu bar.

2.  Click on Link Twitter account. You will be taken to Twitter in a separate browser window/tab. If necessary, sign in. You will then be asked to allow the Profile tool access.

3.  Click the Allow button. Twitter will display a message that you have successfully granted access, along with a PIN you must enter in the CLE.

4.  Enter, or copy and paste, this PIN into the blank field on the Profile Preferences page.

5.  Click the Link button. The CLE will display "Twitter account linked," along with your Twitter user name:


If you no longer want your Twitter account linked, click on the red "X" icon next to your Twitter user name.
