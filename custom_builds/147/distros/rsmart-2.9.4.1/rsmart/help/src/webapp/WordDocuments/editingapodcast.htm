Editing a Podcast

To choose a different file for a podcast or to change its name, description, or available date, access the Podcasts tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Podcasts home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the podcast you want to change and click the Edit link under its name. The CLE displays the Edit Podcast screen. It is essentially the same as the Add Podcast screen.

3.  After changing entries as needed, save your work and return to the Podcasts home page by clicking the Save Changes button. Or, to exit without saving your changes, click Cancel.
