Site Statistics

Generating and Working with Site Statistics Reports

The Site Statistics tool lets you generate and work with reports in several ways. The quickest way to generate a report is to select one from the Overview screen, by clicking on a statistic or a View complete report link in a summary. In addition, you may generate your own customized reports.

You may print any report generated in the Site Statistics tool. You may also export any report as an XLS, CSV, or PDF file.

When you generate customized reports, you may also save and edit them in the CLE. These saved reports may only be accessed from your Reports screen.

To generate a customized report, select the Site Statistics tool and follow these steps:

1.  Click Reports in the Site Statistics menu bar. The CLE displays the Reports screen, where you may access your saved reports and create new ones.

2.  Click the Add button. The CLE displays the New report screen.

  

3.  If you intend to save the report and not just generate it for viewing, printing, or exporting, you will need to give it a title. Click the Show link to enter a title and description.

4.  Choose the type of activity for the report. From the "Activity" dropdown menu, select Visits (default), Events, or Resources.

If you select Events, the CLE displays additional options, allowing you to select by tool, as well as by specific events performed with a particular tool:

5.  Select a time period for the report. If you select Custom from the dropdown menu, the CLE lets you define a specific time period.

6.  Select users for the report. From the dropdown menu, you may select All (default), Role, Group, Custom, or None. Depending on your selection, the CLE displays an additional dropdown menu, so you may further define which users to include.    

7.  Specify how results should be presented in the report. You may select more than one item in the "Totals by" menu, but keep in mind that available items will be determined by the activity you've chosen for the report. You may also set a limit on the number of results.

Use the "Presentation" dropdown menu to specify the format for the report. You may select Table (default), Chart, or Table & Chart.

When you choose "Chart" or "Table & Chart," the CLE displays an additional dropdown menu for selecting the type of chart, as well as more options for defining the information the chart will present:

8.  You now have two options.

a.  Click the Generate report button. The CLE displays the report you have requested.

b.  Click Save report. The CLE saves the report as a link on the Reports screen. You may select a saved report for viewing, printing, or exporting; you may also edit, duplicate, or delete a saved report.

Once you have generated a report in the Site Statistics tool, choose any of the following options:

•      To sort or filter the list, change the number of items displayed on the screen, or move from page to page within the list, proceed as directed under Working with Lists.

•      To see an enlarged view of a chart, click on it. Click Back to return to the report.

•      To print the report, click the Printable version link in the top right corner. When the CLE displays the next screen, click the Send to printer link in the top left and proceed to print as usual for your computer system. Close the window after printing.

•      To export the report to a file on your desktop, click the Export button near the bottom of the screen. When the CLE presents additional options in the form of buttons, select the Export button for the format you prefer. Then open or save the file as usual for your computer system.

•      Click Back to return to: the Overview screen, if you selected a report from a summary; the Reports screen, if you selected a saved report; or the New report screen, if you generated a new customized report.
