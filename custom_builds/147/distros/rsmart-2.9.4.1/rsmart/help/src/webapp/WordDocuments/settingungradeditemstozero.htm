Setting Ungraded Items to Zero

Note: In calculating the course grade to date, the CLE does not include ungraded items, so opting to set all ungraded items to zero (0) may have a dramatic impact on course grades. To undo this action, you must adjust scores manually for each item.      

To have a score of zero (0) automatically entered for all ungraded items, follow these steps:

1.  On the Gradebook home page, click Course Grades in the tool menu bar. The CLE displays the Course Grades screen.

2.  Click the Set Ungraded Items to Zero button near the bottom of the screen. The CLE displays the Set Ungraded Items to Zero screen.

3.  To confirm you want all ungraded items to be scored with a zero (0), click Continue. The CLE displays the Course Grades screen again, along with a confirmation message.
