Introducing the CLE

Authentication

Note: Your authentication process may be slightly different depending on your institution's setup. Your institution might opt to integrate the CLE login with your dominant campus authentication scheme, in which case you do not have to log into the CLE separately from your institution’s portal.

From the gateway, you need to log into the CLE only once in order to access My Workspace and the group sites to which you belong. You do not have to log into each site individually.

Let’s say you are an instructor in English 414 who participates in a cognition research project and has a passion for music. After you log into the CLE, you are authorized to access your own private My Workspace site, the English 414 course site, the Cognition Research project site, and the Music portfolio site (where, perhaps, music majors store audio files showcasing their work).
