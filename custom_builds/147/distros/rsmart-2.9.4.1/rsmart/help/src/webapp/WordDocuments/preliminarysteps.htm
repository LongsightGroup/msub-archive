Forms > Adding a Form

Preliminary Steps

Before creating a form, follow these steps:

1.  Review existing global forms on your CLE instance. You may find a form that suits your purposes, in which case you do not need to add a new one.

2.  Plan the content carefully. Keep the following points mind:

•      Field names are displayed to the left of the text fields to which they apply.

•      Field names wrap somewhat unpredictably.

•      Long field names are difficult for participants to read

3.  Create the .xsd file on which the form will be based. This is a job for an XML expert.

4.  Consider adding the new form in a test CLE instance in order to view and change it as needed before including it in your production CLE instance.
