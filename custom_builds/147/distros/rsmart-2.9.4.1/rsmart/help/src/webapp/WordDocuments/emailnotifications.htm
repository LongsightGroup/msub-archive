Email Notifications

By default, the CLE will send you an email notification when another user does any of the following:

•      Adds you as a connection (sends a connection request)

•      Confirms your connection request

•      Sends you a new message

•      Replies to a message you have sent

•      Posts to your wall

•      Adds you to a project site created with profile connections

Note: In the Profile tool, you may only send messages to, or receive messages from, users who are your profile connections.

To change your notification settings, follow these steps:

1.  Click on Preferences in the Profile tool menu bar.

2.  Under Email notifications, select the appropriate On and Off radio buttons.

3.  Click the Save settings button.
