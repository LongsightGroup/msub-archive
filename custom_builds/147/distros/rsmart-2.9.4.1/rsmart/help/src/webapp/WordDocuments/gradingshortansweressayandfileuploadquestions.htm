Grading Short Answer/Essay and File Upload Questions 

If your test includes non-objective questions, you must grade those questions yourself. To do so, access the Tests & Quizzes tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Tests & Quizzes home page, click the Published Copies tab.

2.  Select Scores from the Action dropdown menu next to the appropriate assessment (this menu item is not displayed until at least one student has taken an assessment and submitted it). The CLE displays the Total Scores screen.

3.  Click on the Questions link at the top of the page

4.  Select the short answer/essay question for which you wish to display all student responses.

5.  For information on accessing a student’s submission for a file upload question, click on the link for the attachment. Or, to see all file uploads for this assessment, see Downloading All Uploaded Files for File Upload Questions.

6.  Review the answers given and enter scores and comments, if desired, for each. You may also attach comments.

7.  Save your work by clicking Update at the bottom of the screen. When the CLE displays the Total Scores screen, the total score for this student has been changed to reflect the grades you have given for each question.

Tip: To apply a score to all students who have not submitted the assessment, enter the appropriate score in the box adjacent to the Apply This Score button, and then click the button.
