Putting a Watch on a Wiki Page

If you choose, the CLE can notifiy you automatically via email whenever the content of any page is changed. To put a watch on pages or to remove the watch from pages, access the Wiki tool and follow these steps:

1.  Click the Watch button in the menu bar.

The CLE displays the Watch screen.

2.  Click to specify your preference for notifications.

3.  To save the setting, click Save. Or, to exit without saving your changes, click Cancel. In either case, the CLE displays the Home page again.

Tip: You may access the Watch screen from any wiki page.
