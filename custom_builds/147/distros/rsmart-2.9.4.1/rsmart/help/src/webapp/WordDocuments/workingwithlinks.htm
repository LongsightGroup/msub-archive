Working with Links

The rich text editor gives you a variety of different linking options. You may link to external web sites, anchors inserted in other parts of the text, and email addresses. In addition, you may link to the following in any site you own:

•      content items (Resources tool)

•      assignments (Assignments tool)

•      assessments (Tests & Quizzes tool)

•      topics or forums (Forums tool)

The way a link provides access for users will depend on what you link to, and how you link to it. For users, clicking on a link may cause a new browser tab/window or email application to open, or a file to be downloaded. You can also insert linked content directly in text, so the content displays for users within the text frame.
