Creating Subsections

You can create subsections and sub-subsections by demoting sections and subsections, respectively. A section or subsection that you demote becomes a subsection of the item directly above it in the list.

To demote one or more sections or subsections, access the Lessons tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Lessons home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Check the box for the section(s) or subsection(s) you want to demote and click the Right link near the top of the screen.
