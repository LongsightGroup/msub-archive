Viewing the Assignment List by Student

As an instructor, you may also view assignment information by student. To do so, follow these steps:

1.  If the Assignments home page is not displayed, click the Reset button or click Assignment List in the menu bar near the top of the page.

2.  From the View dropdown menu, select Assignment List by Student. The CLE displays the Assignment List by Student screen:

3.  Click on the triangle icon for the appropriate student. The CLE displays the submission dates for all submitted assignments and indicates whether an assignment has not been graded (“Ungraded”), graded but not released (“Grade”) or graded and released (“Returned”). The CLE also displays scores for all graded and released submissions.

From this screen, you may also grade an assignment for a particular student, by clicking on the appropriate assignment link and proceeding as instructed in steps 5 – 11 in Viewing and Grading Submissions. Just keep in mind that the "Previous" and "Next" navigation buttons will be inactive.
