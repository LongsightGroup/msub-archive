Revising Your Work

Warning: For work that is to be submitted for evaluation: You may not revise work after submitting it. You may revise work, however, at any time before submitting it for evaluation.

To revise your entries in any of the forms you have completed, follow these steps:

1.  Click the Edit () icon for the form you want to change.

The CLE displays the form.

2.  Make changes as needed and click Save Changes. The CLE displays the screen for the page again. Or, to return to this screen without making changes, click Cancel.
