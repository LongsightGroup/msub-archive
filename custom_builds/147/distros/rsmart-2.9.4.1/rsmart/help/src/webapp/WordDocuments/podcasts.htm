Podcasts

The Podcasts tool allows you to upload audio and video files from your computer system to your site and display these files in a user-friendly way. Site participants may then download these files to the podcaster of their choice (including most computers with podcaster software) in order to play them. Many instructors use podcasts to make audio lectures, video presentations, slideshow presentations, and other material available to students outside of class.

The tool has these features:

•      Supported file types: The Podcasts tool supports many file types, including .mp3, .mp4, and .ppt.

•      RSS feed: An RSS feed allows students to download files to the podcaster of their choice automatically.

•      Association with the Resources tool: The Podcasts tool relies on the Resources tool for storage. Consequently, the default display for the Resources tool includes a folder for Podcasts.

•      Available date: The site organizer may enter a date and time to make a podcast available; participants will not be able to access the file until this date.
