Displaying Total Scores

To see how students performed on a published assessment, access the Tests & Quizzes tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Tests & Quizzes home page, click the Published Copies tab.

2.  Select Scores from the Action dropdown menu next to the appropriate assessment (this menu item is not displayed until at least one student has taken an assessment and submitted it). The CLE displays the Total Scores screen.

This screen provides information about each user. The information you see depends on the assessment setting you specified for students’ identities:

•      If you specified that graders may see students’ identities, the CLE displays a student’s name and user ID, as well as an email link.

•      If you specified anonymous grading, the CLE displays a submission ID only.

In either case, the CLE also displays the following for each user:

•      User role

•      Submit date

•      Score for submission

•      Box in which you may adjust the score

•      Final score that reflects any adjustment

•      Box for providing a comment

On the Total Scores screen, the CLE displays the maximum possible points for the assessment, as well as an Apply This Score button. If you want to apply a score to all students who have not submitted the assessment, enter the appropriate score in the adjacent box and click on the button.  

If you are allowing multiple submissions, the CLE also displays a dropdown menu that lets you choose to see all the submissions from a student, or just the score you’ve selected to record.
