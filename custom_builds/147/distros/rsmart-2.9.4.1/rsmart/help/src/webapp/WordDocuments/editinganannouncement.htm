Editing an Announcement

Note: You may edit only one announcement at a time.

To edit an announcement, access the Announcements tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Announcements list, click the Reset button.

2.  Click the Edit link for the announcement you want to change. (You may also click on the announcement and then click on the Edit link in the menu bar.)

Note: If you do not have permission to edit a particular announcement, you will not see the option to revise it.

3.  Make the desired changes to your announcement. For more information on your options, see Creating an Announcement.

4.  To save your revisions, click Save Changes. Or, to cancel your revisions, click Cancel.

You may also view the revision history for the announcement. To do so, click the See revision history link:


The CLE will list any changes you have made to the email notification and availability settings for the announcement.
