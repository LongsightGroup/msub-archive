Entering a Comment

To enter a comment that will be displayed at the bottom of a page in the wiki, access the Wiki tool and follow these steps:

1.  Navigate to the page in the wiki on which you want to leave your comment.

2.  If necessary, scroll down to the bottom of the page, and then find a comment link in one of these ways:

•      If you’re the first person to enter a comment, click Be the first to comment.

•      If a comment has already been entered, click Add Comment.

•      To a enter a comment as a reply to an existing comment, click Comment next to the name of the person who entered the comment.

In all cases, the CLE displays the New Comment box.

3.  Enter your comment.

4.  To post your comment, click Save. The CLE displays the comment at the bottom of the screen, along with your name and the date and time you posted the comment. (If you entered a comment as a reply to an existing comment, the CLE displays your reply below that comment.) To exit the box without saving your comment, click Cancel.

To hide all comments, click Hide Comments. The CLE hides the comments and displays Show Comments. Click again to see the comments.
