Managing Participants for a Meeting

You can manage the participants for a meeting in several ways:

•      Add a participant to a time slot or wait list

•      Remove a participant from a time slot or wait list

•      Move, replace, or swap participants in a time slot

•      Send an email to meeting participants

To manage participants for a meeting, find the meeting on the Sign-up tool home page and click on its title. The CLE displays the Meeting Details page for that meeting. On this page, you can manage meeting participants.
