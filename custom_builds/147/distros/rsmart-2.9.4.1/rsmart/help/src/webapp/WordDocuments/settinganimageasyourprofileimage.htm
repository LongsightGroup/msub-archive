Setting an Image as Your Profile Image

Tip: You may also opt to use a gravatar as your profile image, as instructed in Setting Preferences in the Profile Tool.  

To set an image from your Pictures gallery as your profile image, access the Profile tool and follow these steps:

1.  Access your Pictures gallery. You may do so in one of two ways:

•      Click on Pictures in the tool menu bar.

•      If you have opted to display your images on your profile, click on the View all pictures link in your profile’s “My Pictures” collection.

In either case, the CLE displays your Pictures gallery, with thumbnails of all the images you have uploaded.

2.  Find the image you want to set as your profile image and click on its thumbnail. The CLE displays a larger view of the image, with two buttons:

3.  Click the Set as profile image button. The CLE displays the following for the image:

4.  If you do not want to set this image as your profile image, click the Cancel button. Otherwise, click the Confirm new profile image button.

The CLE displays your profile with the new profile image.

Note: If you set an image from your Pictures gallery as your profile image and then remove the image from your gallery, that image will not be removed as your profile image. To change a profile image, repeat the above process with a different image, or proceed as instructed in Editing Your Profile.
