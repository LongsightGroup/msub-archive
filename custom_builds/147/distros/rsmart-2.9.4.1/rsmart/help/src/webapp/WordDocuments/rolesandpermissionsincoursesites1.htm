Roles and Permissions in Course Sites

Course sites contain a set of pre-defined roles that users are given when a site is created. Each role has a slightly different set of permissions associated with it. These permissions determine what the users assigned to each role can do with the various tools.

Note: If your local implementation is integrated with a student information system, your course site may be automatically populated with student enrollment data when it is created. If your local implementation is not integrated with a student information system, you or your system administrator must add users to the site and select the appropriate role.

Roles and permissions vary depending on your institution’s requirements. Most often, however, course sites use roles such as Instructor, Teaching Assistant, Student, Guest and Tech Support. For an overview of roles and permissions, see Roles and Permissions in the CLE.
