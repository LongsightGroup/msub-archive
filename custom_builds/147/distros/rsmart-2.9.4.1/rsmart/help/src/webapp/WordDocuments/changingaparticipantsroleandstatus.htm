Changing a Participant's Role and Status

To change a participant’s role and/or status, follow these steps:

1.  Access the Site Editor home page. If necessary, scroll down to find the appropriate participant's name.

2.  To change the participant's role, select the desired role from the Role dropdown list, as shown below.

3.  To update the participant's status, choose either Active or Inactive from the Status dropdown menu. When the status is “inactive,” the user cannot view the site and cannot receive emails sent to the site's e-mail alias.

4.  Click Update Participants. The CLE updates the list to reflect the changes.

Tip: You may also change the status for all participants with a particular role in the site. On the Site Editor home page, select the appropriate role from the dropdown menu located beneath the Update Participants button, and then click either Activate All or Inactivate All.
