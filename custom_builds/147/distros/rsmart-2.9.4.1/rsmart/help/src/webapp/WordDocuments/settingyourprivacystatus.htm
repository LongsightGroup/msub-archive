Setting Your Privacy Status

In the Preferences tool, you may set your privacy status, or site visibility, for either all sites or individual sites to which you belong.

By opting to be hidden or visible in a site, you determine whether site participants will see you in the following:

•      “Users present” list beneath the tools menu

•      Roster tool

•      Recipient list in the Messages tool   

To set your privacy status, access the Preferences tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Preferences menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Privacy Status in the menu bar. The CLE displays the Privacy Status screen.

3.  If you want to set your privacy status for all sites to which you belong, click either Show Me in All Sites or Hide Me in All Sites. The CLE displays a message confirming your privacy status. If you want to set your privacy status for a specific site, proceed to the next step.      

4.  From the dropdown menu, select the appropriate site. The CLE displays privacy status preferences for the site.

5.  Click on the appropriate radio button to select your privacy status preference. To reset to your last saved status, click Ask me again later.

6.  Click Update. The text on the screen reflects your current privacy status.

Note: If you opt to be hidden in a site, you will still be visible to users in certain roles, such as the instructor role.
