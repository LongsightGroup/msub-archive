Changing Evaluation Settings

To edit settings for an in-queue, active, or closed evaluation, access the Evaluation System tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you are not on the Evaluation System home page, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  In the menu bar at the top of the screen, click My Evaluations. The CLE displays the My Evaluations screen.

3.  Find the evaluation you want and click the Edit link for it. The CLE displays the Evaluation Settings screen.

4.  Make changes as needed (see Specifying Settings and Dates, steps 1-6).

5.  To save your work, click Save Settings. The CLE saves your changes and displays the My Evaluations screen again. (Or to return to that screen without making changes, click Cancel.)
