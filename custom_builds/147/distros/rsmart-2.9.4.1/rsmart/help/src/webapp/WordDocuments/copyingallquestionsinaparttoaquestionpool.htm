Copying All Questions in a Part to a Question Pool

You may copy all questions in any part of an assessment to one or more question pools. To do so, display the Questions page for the assessment (see Adding Parts to an Assessment, step 1) and follow these steps:

1.  Find the appropriate part of the assessment and click the Copy to Pool option to the right of its name. The CLE displays the Copy Question page.

2.  Check the box to the left of each pool to which you want to copy all questions in this assessment part.

3.  To save your changes, click Copy. The CLE copies the questions as you directed and displays the Questions page. Or, to return to that screen without copying questions from this part of the assessment, click Cancel.
