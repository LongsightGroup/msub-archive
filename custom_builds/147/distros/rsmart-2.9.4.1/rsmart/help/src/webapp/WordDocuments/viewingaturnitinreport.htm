Viewing a Turnitin Report

If your institution uses the Turnitin service provided by iParadigms, your instructor may send your submission to Turnitin for a report on originality. Your instructor may also choose to let you view the report.

To view a Turnitin report, access the Assignments tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Assignments home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the assignment you want to check and click on it. The CLE displays a screen similar to the one shown below.

3.  If your submission has been processed, you will see the Turnitin icon () and a link to the report. The color of the icon indicates how much text in your submission matches content in the Turnitin database, according to the Turnitin five-color system. Click on the View Report link to access the report.
