Roster

The Roster tool allows you to view and print names, photos, and other information for participants in a site or in a particular group or section within the site. You may also export the list of site participants. The tool is available on course, project, and portfolio sites.

By default, the Roster tool displays a user’s email and name. A user may change this information in either the Account tool or Profile tool, and the changes will be reflected in the Roster tool. A variety of other information that you add in the Profile tool will also be displayed in the Roster tool. For information about working in the Profile tool, see Editing Your Profile.

Note: In the Profile tool, you may opt to hide certain information from users, as instructed in Setting Privacy Controls. The information you choose to hide in the Profile tool will also be hidden from site participants in the Roster tool. In the Preferences tool, you may opt to be hidden in sites, as instructed in Setting Your Privacy Status. If you do, site participants will not see you listed in the Roster tool.
