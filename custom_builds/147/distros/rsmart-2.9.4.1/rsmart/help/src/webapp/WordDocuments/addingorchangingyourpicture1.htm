Adding or Changing Your Picture

To add or change your picture, follow these steps:

1.  If the CLE is not displaying your profile, click the Reset button. Or, click My profile in the menu bar.

2.  Put your cursor into the image box and click on the Change picture link that appears.

3.  Click the Browse button and select the appropriate image from your computer.

Note: You may only upload PNG, JPG, or GIF files, and the file may not be larger than 2 MB.

4.  Click the Upload button. The CLE displays the uploaded image.

Tip: You may also set any image in your Pictures gallery as your profile image, as instructed in Setting an Image as Your Profile Image. In addition, you may opt to use a gravatar (globally recognized avatar) as your profile image, as instructed in Setting Preferences in the Profile Tool.
