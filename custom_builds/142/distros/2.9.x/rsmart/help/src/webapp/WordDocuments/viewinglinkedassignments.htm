Viewing Linked Assignments

In another site to which you belong, your instructor may have opted to link an assignment (in the Assignments tool) to a cell in a matrix. When you submit the assignment, the submission is automatically added to the cell:

When you click on the icon for the submission and then click on the assignment title, the CLE displays a popup window for the submission:

This window always displays the latest status of the submission. If your instructor has graded the submission, for example, the window shows “returned” status, as well as the grade and any comments. If you have resubmitted the assignment, the window displays that submission.

While you may not remove the submission from the matrix cell, you may attach additional materials, as instructed in Adding an Attachment.
