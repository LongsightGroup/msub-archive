Paging through and Searching Long Lists

Some lists can be many pages long. You have the following options for working with them:

•      To change the number of items displayed per page, select from the show pulldown list.

•      To move from page to page within the list, click the appropriate arrow button:

Click  to go to the next page, and  to go to the previous page.

Click  to go to the first page of announcements, and  to go to the last page of announcements.

•      If a search box (two examples follow) is displayed on the screen, enter all or part of the name or other search criteria in the box and click Find or Search. The CLE displays only those items that match the data you entered. To revert to the original screen display, click Clear (this option may not be available in all cases).
