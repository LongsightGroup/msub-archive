Site Statistics

Setting Your Preferences

To specify the tools you want to track activity for and to set other preferences, access the Site Statistics tool and follow these steps:

1.  Click Preferences in the Site Statistics menu bar near the top of the screen. The CLE displays the Preferences screen.

2.  Check boxes and use the dropdown list to indicate your preferences for tools and events processed and for how the CLE displays charts.

3.  To set your new preferences, click Update. Otherwise, click Cancel.

4.  To return to the Site Statistics home page, click the Reset button or click Overview in the menu bar.
