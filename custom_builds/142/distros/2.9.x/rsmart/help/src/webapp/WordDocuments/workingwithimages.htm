Working with Images

In the Profile tool, you may upload images to a Pictures gallery and choose to display those images on your profile so that other users may view them. You may also opt to use any one of those images for your profile image.

Viewing Your Images

There are several ways you may view your images in the Profile tool. If you have opted to display images on your profile, you may view them as thumbnails in a “My Pictures” collection. You may also view thumbnails in your Pictures gallery, by accessing the gallery in one of two ways:

•      Click on the View all pictures link in the “My Pictures” collection on your profile.

•      Click on Pictures in the tool menu bar.

In either case, the CLE will display your Pictures gallery, with thumbnails of all the images you have uploaded:

You may view a larger display of any thumbnail, in either your profile’s “My Pictures” collection or in your Pictures gallery, simply by clicking on it:  

If you click on a thumbnail in your Pictures gallery, you will see buttons for both removing the image and for setting it as your profile image. For further instructions, see Removing an Image and Setting an Image as Your Profile Image.

Viewing Images on Other User Profiles

In some cases, you may also be able to view images displayed on other user profiles. As with your own images, you may view these images in several different ways:

•      View thumbnails in the “My Pictures” collection on a user’s profile. Or, click on the View all pictures link in a “My Pictures” collection to view thumbnails in the user’s Pictures gallery.

•      Click on any thumbnail to view a larger display of the image.

Note: Keep in mind that the images you may view on other user profiles will depend on the privacy and preferences settings of those users. Some users may choose not to display their images on their profiles. Or, they may choose to display images only to their connections. For more information, see Setting Privacy Controls and Setting Preferences in the Profile Tool.
