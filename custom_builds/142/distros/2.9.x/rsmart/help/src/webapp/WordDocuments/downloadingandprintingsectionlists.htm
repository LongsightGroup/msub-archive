Downloading and Printing Section Lists

In the Sections tool, you may download or print a list of students and the sections they belong to. To do so, access the Sections tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Sections menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Student Memberships in the menu bar. The CLE displays the Student Memberships screen.

3.  After sorting or filtering the list as you prefer, click either the Download button or the Print button on the right side of the screen just below the Sections menu bar.

4.  Proceed as usual for downloading to or printing from your computer.
