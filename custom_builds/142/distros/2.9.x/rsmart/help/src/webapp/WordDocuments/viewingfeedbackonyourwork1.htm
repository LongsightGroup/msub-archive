Viewing Feedback on Your Work

One or more members of the site may be assigned to review wizard pages and provide feedback on the work done in them by site participants. If feedback has been offered for one of your wizard pages, you will notice a completed feedback form listed in the page. To read the feedback you have received, follow these steps:

1.  In the Feedback section, click the title of an item in the list.
