Viewing Feedback (as an Instructor)

As an instructor, you may view feedback you have posted for one or all participants in your site. When you access the Feedback tool, the system displays a list of the spreadsheets that are currently posted on the site.

To the right of each listing, the CLE displays options for working with the file. The options are view, view participant, update, delete, and download.
