Editing Scores

You may edit scores for items in one of two ways:

•      Add new scores in the spreadsheet frame, as explained in Entering Grades and Comments in the Spreadsheet Frame.

•      Add new scores in a Student Summary frame, as explained in Entering Grades and Comments in Student Summary Frames.

If scores for an item have been released to students, any changes you make will be reflected in what students see for that item in Gradebook2.
