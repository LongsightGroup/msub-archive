Completing and Attaching Forms

Note: Depending on the design of forms for this wizard, you may need to access multiple screens to provide all of the information required for each form. The instructions below are for the simplest use of forms.

When you are working in a wizard, you may provide information by entering it into online forms or attaching forms you have already completed.

To complete a form, follow these steps:

1.  Click the Add [form name] link for the form. (In the example above, the link is Add Objective.) The CLE displays a screen like this one:

2.  Complete the form and click Save Changes. The CLE saves the information, attaches the completed form to the wizard, and displays the preceding screen again. The name of your newly completed form is shown there.

To attach a form that you have already completed and stored in Resources, follow these steps:

1.  Click the Select [existing item name] link. (In the example above, the link is Select Objective.) The CLE displays the Add Attachment screen.

2.  Select the appropriate instance of the completed form. For information on selecting a form from Resources, see Selecting an Item from Resources for Use in ePortfolios.
