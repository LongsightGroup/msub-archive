Viewing Your Site as a User in Another Role

If you own a course, project, or portfolio site, you may want to see how your site looks from the perspective of a student or a user acting in some other role. To do so, access your site and follow these steps:

1.  Select the appropriate role from the View Site As pulldown list in the upper right corner of the screen.

The CLE displays the site from the perspective of the role you chose. It also replaces the pulldown list with an Exit … View link. For example, if you select the role of student, the link is labeled Exit Student View.

2.  Access tools and materials in the site as desired.

Note: The system continues to display the site from the perspective of the selected role until you either log out or exit the role as described below.

3.  To return to your owner/instructor role, click the Exit … View link.
