Customizing Site Links

The Preferences tool allows you to change the number of site names that are displayed as Quicklinks and allows you to reorder those Quicklinks.

Using the Preferences tool, you may change or reorder your Quicklinks. To do so, click Customize Tabs in the menu bar near the top of the screen and follow the instructions in Changing Your Quicklinks.

You may also use this tool to hide sites and reactivate hidden sites. To do so, click Customize Tabs in the menu bar near the top of the screen and follow the instructions in Hiding Sites and Activating Hidden Sites.
