Live Virtual Classroom

Accessing the Live Virtual Classroom

To access this tool, click Live Virtual Classroom in the tools menu on the left side of your screen. The CLE displays the Live Virtual Classroom home page.

Also known as the Manage Sessions screen, the home page lists all sessions and recordings that are available. For each session, an entry in the Join column shows whether it:

•      is in progress and you may join it (join),

•      is available for replay (watch),

•      has not taken place at the scheduled time (expired), or

•      is scheduled for the future.

You can also see the subject of each session and the name of its moderator. If the event has not yet taken place or is expired, you see its planned duration. If the event has taken place and was recorded, you see the word “recording.”

You may use the standard options for filtering, sorting, and paging through the list of sessions and recordings. For information on working with these options, see Working with Lists.
