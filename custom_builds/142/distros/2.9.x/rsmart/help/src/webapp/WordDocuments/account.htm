Account

Note: If your institution’s instance of the CLE is integrated with another system for external user authentication, you may view, but not edit, your account information in the CLE. You are still permitted to change your password.

The Account tool allows you to change your name, email address, and password. This tool is normally available only in My Workspace.

To access the Account tool, click Account in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Account home page.

This screen provides details about your CLE account.
