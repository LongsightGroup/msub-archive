Reordering Modules

To change the order of the modules in the course, access the Managing Options screen as instructed in Displaying the Managing Options Screen and follow these steps:

1.  Click the Sort link () on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Sorting Modules screen.

The list on the left shows the current order of the modules.

2.  In the box on the right, click a module you want to move.

3.  Clicking on the arrow buttons, move the module to the place you prefer.

        Move to the top of the list.

       Move up the list.

        Move down the list.

       Move to the bottom of the list.

4.  Repeat steps 2 and 3 as needed to move other modules and obtain the desired order. Your changes take effect immediately.
