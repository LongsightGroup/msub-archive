Adding or Changing Your Information

To add or change the information you provide in your profile, follow these steps:

1.  If the CLE is not displaying your profile, click the Reset button. Or, click My profile in the menu bar.

2.  Put your cursor anywhere in the appropriate section and click on the Edit icon that appears:

The CLE displays the appropriate fields for that section:

 

3.  Enter or edit the appropriate information for each section.

4.  To save the information you enter or edit, click Save changes. The CLE displays the appropriate information in that section. If you do not want to save the information, click Cancel.    

On your profile page, you may add or edit the following information.

Basic Information

•      Nickname

•      Birthday—to enter your birthday, click in the field or click on the calendar icon, and then use the calendar that is displayed. Note: You may choose to hide your birth year, as explained in Setting Privacy Controls.

•      Personal summary—use the rich text editor to enter your text. This editor provides limited formatting and lets you add links.

Contact Information

•      Email address

•      Home page URL

•      Home phone

•      Work phone

•      Mobile phone

•      Fax number

Staff Information

•      Position

•      Department

•      School

•      Room

•      Staff profile—enter a profile in the text box. Click on the lower right corner of the box to resize it.

•      University profile URL

•      Academic/research URL

•      Publications and conferences—use the rich text editor to enter your text. This editor provides limited formatting and lets you add links.

Student Information

•      Degree/course

•      Subjects

Social Networking

•      Facebook URL

•      LinkedIn URL

•      MySpace URL

•      Skype username

•      Twitter URL

Personal Information

In this section, the CLE displays separate text boxes for entering favorite books, TV shows, movies, and quotes. Click on the lower right corner of a box to resize it
