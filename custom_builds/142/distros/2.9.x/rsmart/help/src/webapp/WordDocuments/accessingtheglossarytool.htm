Glossary

Accessing the Glossary Tool

To access the Glossary tool, click Glossary in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Glossary home page.

The home page contains a list of glossary terms in alphabetical order, along with their short definitions. You have several options for viewing the glossary:

•      To see the long definition or description of a term, click the term itself. The CLE displays the longer hyperlinked definition in a separate window. To close the window, click Close.

•      To display the first page of terms, click First.

•      To display the previous page of terms, click Previous.

•      To display the next page of terms, click Next.

•      To display the last page of terms, click Last.
