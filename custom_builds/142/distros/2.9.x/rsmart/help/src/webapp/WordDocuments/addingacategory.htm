Adding a Category

To add a new category, access the Discussion Forums tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Discussion Forums home page, click Manage.

2.  In the “Manage” section at the top of the page, click Manage Categories. The CLE displays the Category Listing screen.

3.  Click the Add button, which you will find both above and below the list of categories. The CLE displays the Category Management screen.

4.  Enter a name for the new category.

5.  Optionally, opt to select Enable Grading by Category. If you enable grading, you must also enter maximum allowed points in the Points field. In addition, you may opt to send grades to the Gradebook and Gradebook2 tools by clicking on the checkbox next to Send to Gradebook.

6.  Optionally, specify when the category (and all forums/topics in the category) will first be displayed to students, by entering a date in the Invisible Until field. You may also make an entry in the Due Date/Time field, as well as click the Lock on Due option to lock the category on the specified due date.      

7.  Click Update. The CLE displays the category listing. The new category you have created is at the bottom of the list.
