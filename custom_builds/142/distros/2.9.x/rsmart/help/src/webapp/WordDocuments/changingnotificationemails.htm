Changing Notification Emails

The CLE automatically sends an email to evaluators when your evaluation becomes active. Subsequently, if the Authentication control setting for this evaluation is set to Central Authentication Required, the system also automatically sends a reminder email to evaluators who have not yet responded at intervals you specified in step 13 of Creating Evaluations.

Each email contains default text and appropriate information specific to your evaluation such as evaluation due date, the name of the evaluation, and its URL.

Since these emails are system-generated and contain the information your evaluators need, there is usually no need for you to change them. If desired, however, you may change the default text and key values—that is, the values that represent your system- and evaluation-specific information for either or both emails. To do so, when you reach step 13 of Creating Evaluations, proceed as follows:

1.  Click the link for View/Edit the notification email? or View/Edit the reminder email?, depending on which email you want to change. The CLE displays a screen made up of two sections. The top section contains a list of key values you may want to use in your email.

The bottom half of the email shows the default text with the key values inserted in their default locations.

2.  To change the email, click Edit this Email Template in the bottom half of the window. The CLE displays the Edit Available Email Template screen. (Or, to return to the Evaluations Settings screen without making changes, click Close Window.)

The top half Edit Available Email Template screen repeats the list of key values from the preceding screen. The bottom half contains editable text fields for your email.

3.  Make changes in the subject line and body of the message as needed.

4.  To save your work, click Save Changes and then return to the Evaluation Settings screen by clicking Close Window. (Or, to return to that screen without making changes, click Close Window.)
