About the CLE Screens

A typical screen in the CLE looks like this:

Like most other screens in the CLE, this one contains the following:

•      “Quicklinks” and tabs for accessing your web sites occupy the top portion of the screen, along with a logout area.

•      Under the site name, you see the name of the tool you are using, flanked by a blue Reset button () and a blue Contextual Help button().

•      The tools menu and a “users present” list occupy the left side of the screen.

•      All of these features surround the body of the screen, which contains the fields, data, and buttons you need to perform a specific activity.
