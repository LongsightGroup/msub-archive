Live Virtual Classroom

Viewing a Recorded Session

Shortly after a recorded Elluminate session ends, the CLE automatically adds an entry to the Live Virtual Classroom home page for the new recording. To view this recorded session, access the Live Virtual Classroom tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Live Virtual Classroom home page is not displayed, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  Click the Watch link for the appropriate session. Your computer displays its standard Opening [filename] window.

3.  Proceed as directed in Opening or Downloading an Attachment or Other File.
