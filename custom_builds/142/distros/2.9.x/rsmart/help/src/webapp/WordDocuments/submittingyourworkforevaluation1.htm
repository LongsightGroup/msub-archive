Submitting Your Work for Evaluation

When you have completed your work in a page, it may be appropriate for you to submit it for evaluation.

Warning: Make sure that all of your entries are in their final form and are ready for review and comment before you submit your work in a page or an entire wizard. After you submit your work for evaluation, you cannot access it again to make changes.

To do so, follow these steps:

1.  Click the Submit for Evaluation confirmation button in the Reflection section. The CLE displays a confirmation screen.

2.  To submit the page now, click Submit. Or, to cancel the submission process and return to the screen for this page, click Cancel.
