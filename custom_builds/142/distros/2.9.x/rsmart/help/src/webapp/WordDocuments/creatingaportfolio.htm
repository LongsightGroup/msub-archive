Creating a Portfolio

The process of creating a portfolio involves several steps:

•      Add content and give the portfolio a name. For instructions, see steps 1-8 below.

•      Choose a setting to allow or disallow comments on the portfolio. For instructions, see step 9 below.

•      Specify how and with whom you want to share your portfolio. For instructions see Sharing Your Portfolio.

To begin, access the Portfolios tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Portfolios home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  In the Portfolios menu bar near the top of the screen, click the Create New Portfolio button. The CLE displays this screen.

3.  Choose one of these options.

•      To base your portfolio on an existing template, click the button for the template you want to use. In the example shown above, only one template—Chronological Resume Template—is available for use.

•      To create a portfolio in which you freely choose the layout, text styles, and content to include, click the Design your own portfolio button.

4.  Click Create. Or to return to the home page without creating a portfolio, click Cancel.

After you click Create, the system displays the Summary screen.

This screen is the “hub” screen for the Portfolios tool. It provides many different options for working with your portfolio. You may:

•      Change the name and descriptions of your portfolio.

•      Click links in the top right quadrant of the screen to return to the Portfolios home page (Return to List) or preview your portfolio

•      Click tabs beneath the screen title to move between the Summary, Add/Edit Content, and Share with Others screens.

•      Click buttons and links in the Status, Quick Start Links, and Comments areas to change settings and access important portfolio functions.

Note: If you are using a template to create your portfolio, you may have to supply certain required information before you will be able to share or preview the portfolio.

5.  To change the name of your portfolio, click the Edit link at the top of the screen, enter a meaningful name in the box displayed and click Save Changes. Or, to close the window without changing the name, click Undo.

6.  To enter a description of your portfolio, click the Edit link above the Status box, enter text in the box displayed and click Save Changes. Or, to close the window without entering a description, click Undo.

7.  In the Quick Start Links area, click Add/Edit Content.

8.  Follow the instructions in Creating a Portfolio Based on Your Own Design or Using a Template to Create a Portfolio. The appropriate choice to make at this point follows from the choice you made in step 3.

9.  Click the Summary tab. The CLE displays the portfolio hub screen.

10. In the Comments section on the right side of the screen, click the appropriate button to either allow or disallow comments on your portfolio.
