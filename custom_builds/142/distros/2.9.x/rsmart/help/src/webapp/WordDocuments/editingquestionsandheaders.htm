Editing Questions and Headers

Note: When you change a question or header listed in My Items, your change appears only in the item in the My Items screen, “master” template(s) listed on the My Templates screen that use this item, and evaluations you create from these templates in the future. The system does not apply the change to the question in pre-existing evaluations. To change items in an existing evaluation, see Changing Evaluations.

To change a question or header, access the Evaluation System tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you are not on the Evaluation System home page, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  In the menu bar at the top of the screen, click My Items. The CLE displays the Items screen.

3.  Find the item you want to change, and click the Edit link for it. The CLE displays the Add/Edit Item popup screen.

4.  Proceed as instructed in Adding Questions and Headers, steps 5-9, making changes as needed.
