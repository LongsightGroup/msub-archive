Accessing the Blogs Tool

To access the Blogs tool, click Blogs in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the tool’s home page.

On this page, you’ll see a list of the blogs that other site partipants have created and to which you have access, along with the number of blog entries for each blogger and the date and time of their most recent activity. In addition, the CLE displays an RSS feed icon for each blog.

The CLE also displays the My blog link, for access to all of your own blog entries, as well as the date and time of your last activity.

You may return to the home page at any time by clicking the Reset button or by clicking All the blogs in the Blogs menu bar at the top of the page.
