Searching Users and Requesting Connections

In the Profile tool, you may search profiles of users in the CLE, in order to view their profiles and request connections with them. You may search by user name, email address, or common interest (personal information, such as a favorite book or movie, listed in a user’s profile).

The user profiles returned in your search may include the following:

•      Name, profile image, and status update of each user.

•      A link for viewing each user’s list of connections. If you are not connected with a user, you will also see a link for requesting a connection.  

In addition, you may click on a user name or profile image to view that user’s profile.

Note: What you view in search results will depend on the privacy and preferences settings of the users you search, as well as whether you have connections with those users. Some users may opt to hide various parts of their profile, as well as their list of connections, or they may opt to make certain information available only to their connections. For more information about privacy and preferences settings, see Setting Privacy Controls and Setting Preferences in the Profile Tool.   

To search for users and request a connection, follow these steps:

1.  Access the “Search by name or email” page. You may do so in one of three ways:

•      Click Search in the menu bar.

•      If you have not yet added any connections, click on the Search for connections link in the My Connections section of your profile.

•      Click on Connections in the menu bar. On the My connections page that is displayed, click on the Search for connections link.

2.  Search by name, email address, or common interest:

•      To search by a user’s name or email address, enter the appropriate information in the first field and click on the Search by name or email button.

•      To search by personal information (such as a favorite book or movie) that is listed in a user’s profile, enter the appropriate information in the second field and click the Search by common interest button.

3.  To view a user’s profile, click on the user’s name or profile image. To request a connection with a user, click on the Add as a connection link for that user. The CLE displays a dialogue box:

4.  To send the connection request, click the Add connection button. By default, the CLE will send you a notification email when the user confirms your request. Click the Cancel button if you do not want to send the request.

Once a user has confirmed your connection request, the CLE will display that user’s profile in the My Connections section of your profile, as well as on your My connections page. For more information about connections, see Viewing and Managing Connections.
