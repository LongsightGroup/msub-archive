Sharing Your Portfolio

To share a portfolio with others, access the Portfolios tool and follow these steps:

1.  Choose one of these options:

•      If you are on the Portfolios home page, find the name of the portfolio you want to work with, mouse over the Actions box, and click Share.

•      If you are on the hub screen for the appropriate portfolio, click the Share with Others tab near the top of the screen or click the Share with Others link in the Quick Links section of the screen.

The CLE displays the Share with Others screen.

2.  To share your portfolio with specific individuals or roles:

a.  Click the Click here to share with others link. The CLE displays the Add people to share with screen.

In the default view, this screen lists site members by their names. The list includes site members with whom you are not sharing your portfolio.

Tip: To see a list of all users with whom you are currently sharing your portfolio, press Enter at any time.

Tip: To return to the default view on this screen at any time, click the Select by link and choose browsing for people.  

b.  To share your portfolio with any of the users listed, scroll through the list and click appropriate names. To add these users and remain on this screen, click the Add selected users link above the list. Or, to add the users and return to the Share with Others screen, click the Add and return button at the bottom of the screen.

c.  To share your portfolio by entering a user ID, click the Select by link and choose entering a user id.  Enter a user ID in the box displayed. To add this user and remain on this screen to add another user in the same way, click the Add selected users link. Or, to add the user and return to the Share with Others screen, click the Add and return button at the bottom of the screen.

d.  To share your portfolio with someone by entering an email address, click the Select by link and choose entering an email address.  Proceed as in step c above.

e.  To share your portfolio with all users in a particular role in this particular site or in one or more other sites in your system, click the Select by link and choose picking roles related to this site or picking roles related to all sites. Proceed as in step b above.

3.  After returning to the Share with Others screen, review your list of users and roles to be sure it is correct. Then choose one of these options.

a.  To add more users or roles to the lists, click the Add more people/roles link and proceed as in step 3.

b.  To remove one or more of the users or roles shown, click the appropriate box(es) and then click the Remove selected people/roles link.

4.  To send a notification email to the users with whom you are sharing your portfolio, click the Send automated email message to all of the people with whom you are sharing your portfolio link. The CLE displays a message that notification emails have been sent.

5.  To allow participants with whom you will share your portfolio to add and edit content for the portfolio, check the box for Optionally enable collaborative contributions.

6.  To share your portfolio on the Web with people who are not members of the site:

Warning: Using this option makes your portfolio publicly accessible by anyone with access to the Web.

a.  Check the Everyone on the internet can see it box. If desired, click (Show public URL) to see the URL for your portfolio. 

b.  To copy this link in order to share it with others, right-click the (Show public URL) link and select the option for copying the link location.

7.  After completing your work, click the Summary tab. The system displays the portfolios hub screen. By default, the Active option in the Status box is selected, and the portfolio is being shared with the users you have chosen. If you are not ready to begin sharing the portfolio, select the Inactive option. You may then activate sharing at a later time.
