Mailtool

Changing Your Default Settings

To view and change your default settings for email sent from this tool, access the Mailtool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Mailtool home page is not displayed, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  In the menu bar near the top of the screen, click Options. The CLE displays the Options screen.

3.  Change the defaults as desired by changing the selections. Note the following:

•      If you choose Add to Email Archive, visible to all site participants, all participants will be able to view your emails in the Email Archive tool.

•      If you select Do not allow reply, the default setting for your emails will not permit recipients to reply to your emails. Otherwise, recipients will be able to reply.

•      If you choose Display empty recipient groups, the CLE will display groups that contain no members when you select “Groups” in the “To” section.

4.  To change your default settings and return to the home page, click Update Defaults. Or, to return to the home page without making changes, click Cancel.
