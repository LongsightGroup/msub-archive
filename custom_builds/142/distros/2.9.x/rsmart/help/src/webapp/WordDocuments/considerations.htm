Sections

Considerations

When working with this tool, keep the following points in mind:

•      A participant may not be a member of more than one section in each category. (For example, the student John Doe cannot be a member of both discussion section 1 and discussion section 2.)

•      A section may have any number of teaching assistants (TAs) assigned to it.

•      Depending on permissions, a TA may view and work with information only for students in the section to which this TA is assigned.

•      Instructors can view all announcements, syllabus items, tests, and quizzes posted to all sections.

•      Permissions for each role may be customized at the site level and at the system level, depending on your institution's preference.

•      You will only have access to “auto create sections” if your instance of the CLE has been configured for this feature.
