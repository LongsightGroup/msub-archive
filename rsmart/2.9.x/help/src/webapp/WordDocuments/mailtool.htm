Mailtool

The Mailtool allows you to send email from your course, project, or portfolio site to anyone, whether they belong to your site or not. Using this tool, you may:

•      Choose to send email to all participants in any given role, section, or group, or to individual site participants whom you select. You may also send email to people who are not site participants.

•      Set defaults for your emails, such as allowing reply to sender or allowing no reply, sending a copy to your own email address and/or the Email Archive tool, displaying invalid emails, or displaying empty recipient groups.

•      Override the default settings as desired.
