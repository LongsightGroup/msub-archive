Copying a meeting

To copy a meeting, follow these steps:

1.  On the Sign-up tool home page, find the meeting and click on its title. The CLE displays the Meeting Details page for the meeting.

2.  Click on the Copy button. The CLE displays the Copy this Meeting page. On this page, you’ll see the settings and information from the copied meeting.

3.  Make all necessary changes. For more information, see Adding a Meeting. Keep in mind the following:

•      You cannot change start/end times or meeting type.

•      With the exception of the Publish to Calendar setting, you will not have access to settings in the Other Default Settings list. To access these settings, you must first publish and then modify the meeting, as instructed in Changing Information and Settings for a Meeting.  

•      If you want to preserve participant sign-up from the copied meeting, check the box for Yes, keep all the participant(s) in their corresponding time slots.  

4.  Click the Publish New Meeting button.

The CLE once again displays the Sign-up tool home page, where you’ll see the new meeting listed.
