Viewing Users Present in a Site

Note: Your institution may not have the following feature enabled.

You may be able to see a list of other users who are currently in the site in which you’re working. (When you’re in My Workspace, you only see your own name.)

Depending on how your CLE instance is configured, you may see this list displayed below the site’s tool menu on the left side of the screen. Or, you may see a user icon displayed in the lower right corner of the screen:

The number of users present will be displayed to the right of the icon. You can also click on the icon for a popup window that displays a list of the users present.

Click on the red “X” icon to close the window.
