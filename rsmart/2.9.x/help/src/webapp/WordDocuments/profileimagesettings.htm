Profile Image Settings

In the Profile tool, you can make opt to use your gravatar (globally recognized avatar) as your profile image.

Note: In order for the CLE to display a gravatar as your profile image, you must provide a gravatar image for the email address that is associated with your CLE account and displayed in your profile. If you have not provided an image for this email address, the CLE displays a generic gravatar image () by default.  

To use your gravatar as your profile image, follow these steps:

1.  Click on Preferences in the Profile tool menu bar.

2.  Under Profile image settings, check the box for Use my gravatar as my profile image.

3.  Click Save settings.

Your profile image is now the gravatar image you provided for the email address associated with your CLE account and displayed in your profile.
