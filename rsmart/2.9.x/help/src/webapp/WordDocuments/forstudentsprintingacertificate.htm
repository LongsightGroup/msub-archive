For Students: Printing a Certificate

As a student, you may print a certificate an instructor has made available, if you have met the gradebook criteria for printing the certificate. This criteria may include a certain score or the due date for one or more gradebook items, as well as your course grade.

There are two ways to access a certificate for printing:

•      Visit the appropriate site and select the Certification tool. Then, for the appropriate certificate, click the Check Status link.

•      Click on a status link provided by your instructor.

In either case, what you see depends on whether you have met all the criteria for printing the certificate. If you have not met all the criteria, the CLE displays the specific criteria that must still be met:


If you have met all criteria, you’ll see the Print Certificate page:


On this page, click the Print button. A PDF version of the certificate will be downloaded to your computer. If you do not want to print the certificate at this time, click Cancel. The CLE displays the tool home page again, along with a Print Certificate link for the certificate. You may always return to the tool at a later date, or again click on the link provided by your instructor, to print the certificate.
