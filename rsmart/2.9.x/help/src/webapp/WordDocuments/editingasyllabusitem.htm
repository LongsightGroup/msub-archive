Editing a Syllabus Item

To edit a syllabus item, access the Syllabus tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Syllabus menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Create/Edit on the Syllabus menu bar.

3.  Click the name of the syllabus item you want to change.

The CLE displays the current content of the syllabus item in the Edit Syllabus Item screen. It is essentially the same as the Add Syllabus Item screen.

4.  Follow steps 4 - 7 of Creating a Syllabus Item.
