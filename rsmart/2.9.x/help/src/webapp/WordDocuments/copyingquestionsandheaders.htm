Copying Questions and Headers

When you want to create a new question or header, you may want to copy an existing item and then edit the copy. To copy a question or header, access the Evaluation System tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you are not on the Evaluation System home page, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  In the menu bar at the top of the screen, click My Items. The CLE displays the Items screen.

3.  Find the item you want to copy, and click either of the two Copy links for it. The CLE displays the copied question or header at the bottom of the list of items.
