Viewing Your Site as a User in Another Role

If you are a site owner, you may want to explore how your site looks from the perspective of a student or a user in some other site role.

To do so, select the appropriate role from the View Site As dropdown menu in the upper right corner of the screen.

Once you select a role, the CLE displays the site from the perspective of that role. In addition, the dropdown menu is replaced with an exit link. If you selected the student role, for example, you would see an Exit Student View link:

To exit the perspective of another site role, click on the exit link.

Note: When you use  “View Site As,” the CLE continues to display the site from the perspective of the selected role until you either exit the role or log out of the CLE.
