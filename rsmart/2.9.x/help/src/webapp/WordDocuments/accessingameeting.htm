Accessing a Meeting 

When you click on a meeting title, the CLE displays the Meeting Details page for that meeting. For a meeting with multiple time slots, the page may look similar to this:

On this page, you’ll see:

•      General information about the meeting, which you may hide by clicking the Hide meeting info above link. If the meeting is recurring, you’ll see an icon () displayed next to the meeting title. To view the sites or groups for which the meeting is available, click on the Show site(s)/groups(s) details link. (In some cases, a meeting organizer may make a meeting available to more than one site, or to specific groups in a site.)

•      All time slots for the meeting and, in the Available Places column, the number of participants who may sign up for each slot. If the slot is full, “None” will be displayed. If participants are allowed to join a wait list for a slot, the number of participants on the wait list will be displayed. A “locked” slot is not available for sign-up and its sign-up button will be grayed out.

•      Other participants who have signed up for the meeting, in the Participants column. Keep in mind that the site owner may decide to hide the identities of participants, in which case you will see “Private” in this column.

•      Your sign-up status, in the Your Status column. If you have not yet signed up, you’ll see nothing. If you have signed up, you’ll see “Signed up.” If you have joined a wait list, you’ll see “On Wait List.” Put your cursor over this label to see your ranking in the wait list.

Note: Once you join the wait list for a slot, you will be automatically signed up when a place opens. Availability depends on your rank in the list and the number of available spaces.

•      Buttons for taking a particular action, in the Action column. If you have not yet signed up, you’ll see the Sign Up button for each available slot and a Join Wait List button for each slot that is full. If you have signed up for a slot, or if you have been assigned to a slot, you’ll see a Cancel Sign-up button for the slot. If you have joined or been added to a wait list for a slot, you’ll see a Remove from Wait List button for the slot.

For a meeting that does not have multiple time slots, you’ll see all of the above displayed in a single row.

On this page, you can also:

•      Click on Export as Excel in the menu bar to export meeting information in Excel (.xls) spreadsheet format. See Exporting Meetings.

•      Click on Print to print meeting information. See Printing a Meeting.

•      Click on the calendar icon () that displays in the Your Status column when you sign up, to download the meeting in iCal format. See Downloading a Meeting in iCal Format.
