Overriding Clog Preferences

You may override your global Clog notification preference, to apply a different preference for specific sites to which you belong. To do so, access the Preferences tool and follow these steps:

1.  Click Notifications in the Preferences menu bar. The CLE displays the Notifications page.

2.  Scroll down to the Blog section and click Add a site.

The CLE displays all sites with the Clog tool in which you are a participant.

3.  Click on the checkbox for the appropriate site(s), and then click Add Site(s). On the Notifications page, the CLE displays a separate notification dropdown menu for each selected site:

4.  Select the appropriate notification option from the dropdown menu(s).

5.  To save your changes, click Update Preferences. The CLE displays the Preferences home page again, with a message that your changes have been updated successfully. Or, to return to the home page without saving your changes, click Cancel Changes.

To remove the notification override for a site, click the “remove” icon ( ) next to the dropdown menu for that site.
