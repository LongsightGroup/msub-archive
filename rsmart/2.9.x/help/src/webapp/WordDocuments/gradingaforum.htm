Grading a Forum

To grade student participation for a particular forum, follow these steps

1.  On the Forums home page, click the More link for the appropriate forum.

2.  From the displayed menu, select Grade. The CLE displays a Statistics & Grading page for that forum.

Tip: You can also access the Statistics & Grading page by first clicking Statistics & Grading in the tool menu bar, next clicking Statistics & Grading by Topic, and then selecting the appropriate forum from the forums column.    

What you see on this page will depend on whether you’ve already associated the forum with a gradebook item, as instructed in step 9 of Adding a Forum.

•      If the forum is already associated with a gradebook item, you’ll see a column for entering scores, as well as a column for entering comments. By default, the comment boxes will be hidden, but they will display if you click Show Comments.

•      If the forum is not yet associated with a gradebook item, the columns for scores and comments will not display. Instead, a Grade link will display for each student.

You can always associate the forum with a gradebook item—or change an existing association—by making the appropriate selection from the Select a gradebook item dropdown menu.

Or, if you haven’t yet associated the forum with a gradebook item, you can click the Grade link for each student. In the popup window that displays, you can select from the Gradebook Items dropdown menu.

On the Statistics & Grading page, you can view all messages a student has authored in the forum by clicking on the appropriate user name. When you do, the CLE displays a popup window:

In this window, you'll see a word count for each message. You can sort messages by topic or date. You can also print all messages, by clicking on the print icon (). To close the popup window, click the Close Window button.   

3.  Grade participation in the forum. You may only enter grades as points, and only to the maximum allowed for the associated gradebook item. You can grade in one of two ways:

•      If you have associated the forum with a gradebook item, enter scores and comments in the appropriate fields. To apply one score to all students without a grade, enter the score in the displayed field and click Apply Grade to all Ungraded. Lastly, click Submit Grades. The CLE displays a confirmation message.

•      If you have not yet associated the forum with a gradebook item and want to grade each student separately, click the Grade link for the appropriate student. In the displayed popup window, first associate the forum with a gradebook item. Then, enter a score and comments as appropriate and click Submit Grade. The popup window closes.

To return to the home page, click the reset button (). You can also access other forums and topics for grading by clicking the Statistics & Grading link in the navigation breadcrumb.
