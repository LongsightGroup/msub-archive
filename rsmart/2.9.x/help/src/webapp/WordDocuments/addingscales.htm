Adding Scales

While you’ll find a variety of existing scales to choose from when adding a rating scale question, you may also add your own scales. To add a rating scale, follow these steps:

1.  If you are not on the Evaluation System home page, click the Reset button to display it.

2.  In the menu bar at the top of the screen, click My Scales. The CLE displays the Scales screen.

 

     This screen displays all of the rating scales you may access from the “Scale”
     dropdown menu when adding a rating scale question. The list is sorted
     alphabetically by scale title and includes both existing scales and scales
     you have created.

Note: You may edit, remove, and copy any scale you have created, but you may only copy existing scales. To work with an existing scale, first copy it, and then edit as desired.

3.  Click Add new scale. The CLE displays the Add/Edit Scale screen.

4.  In the Scale Title field, enter a title for the scale.

5.  Add scale items in the displayed fields. For additional fields, click Add Scale Point. To remove a scale item, click the Remove button next to the appropriate item.

6.  Optionally, indicate which scale item represents the ideal response. Positions you may select are based on both vertical and horizontal formats.

7.  Click Save Scale. The CLE displays the Add/Edit Scale screen, with your new rating scale included in the list of scales.

The new scale will also be displayed in the Scale dropdown menu when adding a rating scale question.
