Managing Groups

Note: When you assign students to a group, those students may come from the same or different sections. Being able to cross sections when creating groups is a powerful feature. It allows you to set up collaborative group projects across diverse student populations and types of classes (which may include online, hybrid, traditional, and other types of classes).

 

Note: A student may belong to more than one group.

You may add, change, or delete a group from your site. To manage groups, access the Site Editor tool and follow these steps:

1.  If you do not see the Site Editor menu bar, click the Reset button.

2.  Click Manage Groups on the Site Editor menu bar. The CLE displays the Group List screen.

This screen contains a list of the groups on your site.
