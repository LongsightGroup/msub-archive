Changing Information and Settings for a Meeting

Note: You may not modify the meeting type for an existing meeting. If the meeting has customized time slots, you may not change its start or end time.

To change the information or settings for a meeting, follow these steps:

1.  On the Sign-up tool home page, find the meeting and click on its title. The CLE displays the Meeting Details page for the meeting.

2.  Click on Modify in the menu bar at the top of the page. The CLE displays the Modify Meeting page for the meeting.

3.  If the meeting is recurring, you’ll see a Modify Options section at the top of the page. By default, the Modify all future recurring meetings setting is selected. If you only want to modify the current meeting, select Modify current meeting only. Keep in mind that the meeting will become the single-slot type if you select this setting.

4.  Make all necessary changes. For more information, see Adding a Meeting.

5.  Click Publish Modification to save your changes.

When you save your changes, the CLE once again displays the Sign-up tool home page.
