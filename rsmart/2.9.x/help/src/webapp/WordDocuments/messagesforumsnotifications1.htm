Messages & Forums Notifications

Your My Workspace home page may also contain a Messages & Forums Notifications section. In this section, the CLE notifies you of new messages in the Messages tool, as well as new posts in the Forums tool, in any site to which you belong.

Note: You will not see new messages or Forums posts for sites that you’ve hidden using the Preferences tool. For more information, see Activating Hidden Sites.  

For each site, you’ll see the number of unread messages and  posts.

To read new messages or posts in a site, click on the appropriate number. Depending on what you’re accessing, the CLE displays the home page of either the Messages or Forums tool in that site.

You can also remove sites for which you’re seeing notification of new messages or posts. To do so, click the Options button. The CLE displays the list of sites for which you’re seeing notification:

To remove a site or sites from this list, check the appropriate box. Then, click Update.
