Viewing Grades

In Gradebook2, you may view grades in two different ways:

•      By Viewing Grades in the Spreadsheet Frame, you may view all grades for all students.

•      By Viewing Grades in a Student Summary Frame, you may view all grades and comments, as well as the course grade, for a given student.
