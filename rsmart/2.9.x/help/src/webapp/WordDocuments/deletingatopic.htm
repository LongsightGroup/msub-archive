Deleting a Topic

Warning: When you delete a topic, you also delete all messages in it.

To delete a topic, access the Forums tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Forums home page, find the appropriate topic.

2.  Click the More link for the topic. From the displayed dropdown menu, select Delete Topic. The CLE displays this message:

3.  To proceed to delete the topic and its contents, click the Delete Topic button. You will return to the Forums home page.

Tip: You can also delete a topic by clicking the Topic Settings link for it on the Forums home page, and then clicking the Delete Topic button at the bottom of the Topic Settings page.
