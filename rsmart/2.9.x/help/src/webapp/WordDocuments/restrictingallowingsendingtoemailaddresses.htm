Restricting/Allowing Sending to Email Addresses

As a site owner, you may change the setting that controls whether users have the option to send copies of messages to recipients’ email addresses. The setting controls this option for all users in your site. You may want to enable it if users don’t check messages on this site often. You may want to disable it to avoid overloading your institution’s email system.

To turn this setting on or off, access the Messages tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Messages home page, click the Settings link in the menu bar. The CLE displays the Messages – Settings screen.

2.  Choose one of the following options:

•      To allow users to send copies of messages to the recipients’ email addresses, click the Yes option in the top set of radio buttons.

•      To turn this capability off, click No.

3.  To save the new settings, click the Save Settings button. Or to exit without changing the settings, click Cancel. The CLE displays the Messages home page.
