Assigning a Teaching Assistant (TA)

After you have created sections, you may add TAs to them.  

Note: In order to assign TAs to a section, at least one site participant must have the appropriate TA role. You may assign a TA to multiple sections. To assign the TA role to one or more participants, see Adding Participants.

To assign a TA or TAs to a section, access the Sections tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Sections home page, click the Assign TAs link for the appropriate section.

The CLE displays the Assign Teaching Assistants screen for that section.

2.  Select the TA(s) to be assigned to the section, in one of two ways:

•      Select all TAs by clicking the button with two right-facing arrows () under Move All. The entire list of TAs is moved to the box on the right.

•      In the box on the left, select a specific TA, or select multiple TAs by holding down your CTRL key (Windows) or Command key (Mac) while clicking on the appropriate users. Then, click the button with one right-facing arrow (). You’ll see the selected TA(s) in the box on the right.

3.  To assign the selected TA or TAs to the section, click Assign TAs. The CLE displays the Sections home page, along with a confirmation message:

On the home page, you’ll see the assigned TA(s) for the section listed in the Teaching Assistant (TA) column.
