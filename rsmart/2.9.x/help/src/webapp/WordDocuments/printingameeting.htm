Printing a Meeting

To print a meeting, follow these steps:

1.  On the Sign-up tool home page, find the meeting and click on its title. The CLE displays the Meeting Details page for the meeting.

2.  Click on Print in the menu bar.

3.  Proceed to print as you typically would on your computer.

The document you print will include meeting details, such as location and date/time, as well as time slots and participant sign-up and wait list.
