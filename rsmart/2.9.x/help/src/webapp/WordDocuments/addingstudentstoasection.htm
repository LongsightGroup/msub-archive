Adding Students to a Section

Note: You may not add students to a full section.

After you have created sections, you may add students to them. To add students to a section, access the Sections tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Sections home page, click the Assign Students link for the section to which you want to add students.

The CLE displays the Assign Students screen for that section.

2.  If you want to reassign students from another section of the same category, select the appropriate section from the dropdown menu, and you’ll see the students assigned to that section listed in the box on the left. Otherwise, keep the default Unassigned Students menu selection, so you’ll see the list of unassigned students displayed in this box.

3.  Select the students to be assigned to the section, in one of two ways:

•      Select all students by clicking the button with two right-facing arrows () under Move All. The entire list of students is moved to the box on the right.

•      Select specific students in the box on the left, by holding down your CTRL key (Windows) or Command key (Mac) while clicking on the appropriate students. Then, click the button with one right-facing arrow (). The selected students are moved to the box on the right.

4.  To assign the selected students to the section, click Assign students. The CLE displays the Sections home page, along with this confirmation message:

Or, to return to the home page without assigning students, click Cancel.
