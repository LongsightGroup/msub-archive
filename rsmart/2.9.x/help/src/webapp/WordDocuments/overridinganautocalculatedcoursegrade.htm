Overriding an Auto-calculated Course Grade

To override a course grade for a student, access the Gradebook tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Gradebook home page, click Course Grades in the tool menu bar. The CLE displays the Course Grades screen.

2.  In the Grade Override column, enter a grade in the appropriate box.  

Note: The CLE only accepts entries in the grade format for which you have set up the gradebook. For example, if the course is graded on a simple letter grade scale, you may enter only the letters A, B, C, D, or F. If the course is graded on a standard letter grade scale, you may add a + (plus sign) or - (minus sign) to the letter grade. If the course is graded on a pass/fail scale, you may enter only the letters P or NP.

3.  To save your changes, click Save Changes. The CLE displays the Course Grades screen again, along with a confirmation message. Or, to cancel your changes, click Clear Changes. The CLE displays the original data on the screen.

If you want to revert to an existing course grade, access the Course Grades screen and delete the grade you entered in the Grade Override column. Then, click Save Changes.

To display course grades to students now, access the Gradebook tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Gradebook home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  In the menu bar near the top of the screen, click Course Grade Options. The CLE displays the Course Grade Options screen.

3.  Check the Display course grade to students now box.

4.  Save this setting by clicking the Save button at the bottom of the screen. Or, to exit without saving your work, click Cancel. The CLE displays the Gradebook home page.
