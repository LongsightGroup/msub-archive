Widget Settings

The widget settings allow you to control the display of your “kudos” rating, pictures, and online status for your profile. To change your widget settings, follow these steps:

1.  Click on Preferences in the Profile tool menu bar.

2.  Under Widget settings, make appropriate changes to the following settings:

•      Click on the checkbox for Show my kudos rating to display the rating. This daily rating for your profile is based on various factors, such as the amount of information you have provided, whether you display a profile image, and the number of status updates you have posted.      

•      Click on the Show my pictures checkbox to display images you have uploaded to your Pictures gallery. The images will be displayed on your profile in a “My Pictures” collection, directly below your profile connections.

•      Click on the checkbox for Show my online status if you want to indicate whether you’re currently logged in to the CLE. A dot next to your name displays in gray if you’re not available and in green if you’re online. Users may also click on the dot for a message indicating your online status.

If you enable this widget, you can also specify which users are able to check your status, by selecting the appropriate privacy setting. For more information, see Setting Privacy Controls.

3.  After you have selected the appropriate settings, click Save settings to save your changes.
