News

The News tool allows you to view information from a news source, blog, events listing, or other site that updates its content dynamically.

The number of News tools for a site is specified during site setup, but as a site owner or instructor, you may add more News tools later by using the Edit tools option in the Site Editor tool. Each instance of the News tool may be given a unique name such as China Business News, Yahoo News, etc.
