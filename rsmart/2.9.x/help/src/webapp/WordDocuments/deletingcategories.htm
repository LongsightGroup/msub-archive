Deleting Categories

You may delete a category only if it has no forums in it. To delete forums, see Deleting Forums.

To delete a category, follow these steps:

1.  On the Discussion Forums home page, click Manage.

2.  In the “Manage” section at the top of the page, click Manage Categories. The CLE displays the Category Listing screen.

3.  Locate the category you want to delete and check the box for it:

To delete more than one empty category, check other boxes, as appropriate.

4.  Click Delete Selected. The CLE deletes the category.

Warning: The CLE does not ask you to confirm the delete action. Instead, it deletes the selected category immediately.
