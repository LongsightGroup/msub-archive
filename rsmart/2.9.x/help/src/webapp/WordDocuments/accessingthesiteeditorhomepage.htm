Site Editor

Accessing the Site Editor Home Page

To access the Site Editor tool, click Site Editor in the tools menu on the left side of the screen. The CLE displays the Site Editor home page.

The Site Editor home page provides a summary of basic information about your site. From this page, you can administer your site by using the buttons located on the Site Editor menu bar. You may also do the following:

•      View and print a list of participants in your site.

•      If your site has groups, view and print the list of participants for each group.

•      Change a participant's role and status on the site, change the status of all participants with a particular role on the site, and delete all or selected participants from the site.
