Hiding a Portfolio

You may hide any portfolio that you are allowed to view. Many people find this feature handy if they have many portfolios to review and want to hide those they have already seen. To hide a portfolio, access the Portfolios tool and follow these steps:

1.  If the Portfolios home page is not displayed, click the Reset button.

2.  Find the portfolio you want to hide, mouse over the Actions pulldown list, and click Hide. The CLE changes the link to Show.

3.  To show this portfolio whether it has been reviewed or not, click mouse over the Actions pulldown list, and click Show.
