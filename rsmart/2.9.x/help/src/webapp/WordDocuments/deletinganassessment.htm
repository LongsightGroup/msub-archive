Deleting an Assessment

You may delete an unpublished version of an assessment at any time, even if you have published the assessment. (A published assessment is a copy of the unpublished version).

To delete an assessment, access the Tests & Quizzes tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Tests & Quizzes home page, find the assessment you want to delete.

2.  Select Remove from the Action dropdown menu next to the title of the assessment. The CLE displays a confirmation screen.

3.  To delete the assessment, click Remove. The CLE displays the Tests & Quizzes home page, and the title of the assessment you removed is no longer displayed in the list. Or, to return to that screen without deleting the assessment, click Cancel.
