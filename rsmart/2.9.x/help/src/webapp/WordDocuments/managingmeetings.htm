Managing Meetings

To manage a meeting you have created, first locate the meeting on the Sign-up tool home page and click on its title. The CLE displays the Meeting Details page for that meeting:      

From this page, you may view information and settings for the meeting. You may also:

•      Change information and settings

•      Copy the meeting

•      Export the meeting

•      Print the meeting

•      Download the meeting in iCal format

•      Lock or delete a time slot for the meeting

•      Synchronize group membership

•      Manage meeting participants

In addition, you may view comments added by meeting attendees upon sign-up, by putting your cursor over, or clicking on, the appropriate user name.
