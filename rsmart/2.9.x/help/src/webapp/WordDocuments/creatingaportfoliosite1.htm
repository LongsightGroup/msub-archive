Creating a Portfolio Site

Warning: When you create a site, you cannot exit in the middle of the procedure, save your work, and continue later. Therefore, it is recommended that you obtain all of the necessary information before beginning the procedure.

In the Site Setup tool, you can create a portfolio site in one of two ways:

•      If you have a good understanding of the tools available for use in portfolio sites and are willing to choose tools as part of the site creation process, you may build your own portfolio site, as instructed in Building Your Own Portfolio Site.

•      If you are new to the CLE tools or do not want to select tools for this site from a list, you may create a site from a template. A template eliminates the need for you to understand the full range of tools by providing a set of appropriate tools for your new site. Proceed with this option as instructed in Creating a Portfolio Site from a Template.

Note: Regardless of which site creation approach you choose, you may add and delete tools at any time after creating your site.
