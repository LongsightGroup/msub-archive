Linking Directly to a Message

In some cases, you may want to copy the URL for a message, so you can provide other site participants with a direct link to the message. To do so, follow these steps:

1.  Access the message. Find it in the appropriate conversation in the appropriate topic, and then click on its subject so the CLE displays only that message.

2.  In the menu bar, click on Copy link. The CLE displays the highlighted URL for the message in the copy link box.

3.  Copy the URL. To close the copy link box, click the “x” in the corner.   

You can then provide the URL for the message to other site participants. When they click on the URL, they will be taken directly to the message.
