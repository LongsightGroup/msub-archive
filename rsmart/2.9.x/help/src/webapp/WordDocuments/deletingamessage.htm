Deleting a Message

Only instructors and site organizers may delete messages in discussion forums. Ordinarily, you would take this step only if a post is offensive.

To delete a message, access the appropriate forum and topic, and follow these steps:

1.  Click the X button for the desired message.

The CLE displays this confirmation window:

2.  To proceed, click OK. The CLE deletes the message and displays the topic with its revised list of messages.

Or, to exit without deleting the message, click Cancel.

Warning: After you click OK, your message is erased and cannot be retrieved.
