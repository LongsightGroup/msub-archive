Managing Profile Connections

Note: You can also manage connections by accessing the Profile tool. For more information, see Viewing and Managing Connections.

You may request, confirm, ignore, or remove a connection with another site participant by accessing that participant’s profile in the Clog tool.  

To request a connection with another site participant, follow these steps:

1.  Access the participant’s profile, as instructed in Viewing Profiles.

2.  In the user’s profile, click on the Add as a connection link. When the user confirms the request, you’ll then see the Remove connection link.    

To confirm or ignore a connection request from another site participant, follow these steps:

1.  Access the participant’s profile, as instructed in Viewing Profiles.

 

2.  To confirm the request, click the Confirm connection request icon (). To ignore the request, click the Ignore connection request icon ().

To remove a connection with another site participant, follow these steps:

1.  Access the participant’s profile, as instructed in Viewing Profiles.

2.  Click the Remove connection link. You will no longer have a connection with that user, and the Add as a connection link will be displayed.
