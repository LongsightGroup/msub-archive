Assigning Groups of Evaluators

Note: After an evaluation has become active, you may not de-select, change, or add groups of evaluators.

You may assign all authorized participants in one or more sites to take your evaluation. You also have the option of creating and assigning ad hoc groups to take the evaluation. You may choose these groups of evaluators at either of two points:

•      As step three of creating a new evaluation (see Creating Evaluations), immediately after completing step two (see Specifying Settings and Dates), or

•      any time within 5 calendar days of exiting the creation process without completing the evaluation. In this case, access the evaluation by clicking the Continue creating this evaluation link for it in the Partially Complete Evaluations section of the My Evaluations screen. At the bottom of the Evaluation Settings screen, click Continue to Assign to Courses. 

Warning: If you do not complete a partial evaluation within 5 calendar days, the system deletes it.

In either case, the system displays the Edit Evaluation Assignment screen.

Proceed as follows:

1.  To see all available groups, click both blue arrows. The CLE displays two lists.

2.  If needed, create a new ad hoc group as follows:

a.  Click Create a new Adhoc Group now. The CLE displays the Creating a New Adhoc Group screen.

b.  Enter a descriptive name for this group in the Group Name field.

c.  In the large white box, enter either the CLE username or email address of each person you want to include in the group. Enter each name on a separate line.

d.  Click Create Adhoc Group. The CLE displays Modifying Adhoc Group screen. (Or to return to the previous screen without adding this group, click Back to Evaluation Group Assignments.)

e.  To delete any of the users you have added, click the X button by the name(s).

f.   To add more users, proceed as in steps 2c and 2d.

g.  Return to the Edit Evaluation Assignment screen by clicking Back to Evaluation Group Assignments.

3.  Select the group or groups of evaluators you want to assign this evaluation to by clicking the box for each appropriate group.

Note: You may need to reopen the expanded lists of groups by clicking the blue arrows again.

4.  To proceed, click Save Assigned Groups. The CLE displays the Evaluation Assignment screen. (Or to return to the preceding screen, click Back.)

This screen presents a summary of the dates and groups you have selected.

5.  To complete your work, click Done. Or to change the group assignments, click Change Assigned Groups and repeat steps 1-4.
