Removing CLE Access to a Google Account

Note: Removing CLE access permission to a Google account will affect all sites in which you have added links to documents in that account. The links will remain available in Resources, but users will not be able to access the content unless you restore permission.    

To remove CLE access permission to a Google account, follow these steps.

1.  In My Workspace, select the Linked Accounts tool from the tools menu. The CLE displays the following screen.

2.  Check the box next to “Google Docs” and then click Delete in the menu bar. You’ll see the following window:

3.  Click the OK button in this window. The CLE will no longer display “Google Docs” in the Provider’s Name column.

To restore CLE access to your Google account, proceed as directed in steps 1 and 2 in Adding Links to Google Documents.
