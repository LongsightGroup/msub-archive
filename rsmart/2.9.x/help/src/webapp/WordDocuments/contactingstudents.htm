Contacting Students

You may contact any student who is expected to submit an assessment by sending him or her an email. To do so, access the Tests & Quizzes tool and follow these steps:

1.  On the Tests & Quizzes home page, click the Published Copies tab.

2.  Select Scores from the Action dropdown menu next to the appropriate assessment. The CLE displays the Total Scores screen.

3.  Click the Email link under a student’s name to send an email message to that student.
