Viewing Profiles

You may access the profile information of another site participant in several ways:

•      On the home page, locate a post by the participant and put your cursor over the participant’s name. The user’s profile information will be displayed in a popup box.

  

•      Click on View authors in the menu bar for a list of all site participants. You can put your cursor over a participant’s name, so the profile displays in a popup box. Or, you can click on the name. The profile will be displayed above the list of posts by that user.

Note: The profile information you see displayed for a particular user will depend on the content the user provided in the Profile tool, the privacy settings selected by the user for that tool, and whether you have a connection with the user.
