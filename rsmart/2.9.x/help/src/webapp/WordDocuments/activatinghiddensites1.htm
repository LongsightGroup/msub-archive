Activating Hidden Sites

You may want to active one or more hidden sites, so so they can display as site links and will be included in a list of active sites to which you can navigate (as explained in Navigating to a Site).

Tip: You may always access hidden sites from the home page of the Site Setup tool in My Workspace.

To activate a hidden site or sites, follow these steps:

1.  Access the Customize Tabs page in the Preferences tool. You can do so in one of the following ways: 

•      In My Workspace, select the Preferences tool. Then, click on Customize Tabs in the menu bar.

•      Depending on how your CLE instance is configured, you can also access the Customize Tabs page by clicking Edit to the right of your site links, or by clicking Preferences in the sites drawer (see Navigating to a Site) or the user menu (see Navigating with the User Menu).

Hidden sites that can be activated will be listed in the My Hidden Sites box.

2.  From the My Hidden Sites list, select the site or sites you want to hide. You can do so in one of two ways:

•      To select a specific site or sites, click on the site(s) and then click the right () arrow to move.

•      To activate all hidden sites listed, click the double right arrow ().

The site or sites will display in the My Active Sites box.

3.  To save your changes, click Update Preferences.
