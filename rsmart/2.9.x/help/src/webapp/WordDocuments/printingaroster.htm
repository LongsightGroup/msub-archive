Printing a Roster

The Roster tool allows you to print your site roster in either overview or pictures mode. You may want to choose a specific View option, and sort the list before printing it.

To print a roster, access the Roster tool, change the display as desired, and follow these steps:

1.  Click the Print button.

2.  When your computer system displays its print window, proceed as usual for printing from your system.
