Introducing the CLE > My Workspace

My Workspace Information

On the My Workspace home page, the My Workspace Information section displays general information from your system administrator. You cannot change the text in this section, but you can change the title of the section, as well as the height of the frame in which it is displayed. To do so, follow these steps:

1.  In the My Workspace Information section, click the “edit” icon (). The CLE displays the following settings options.

2.  Change the entry in the Title box, if desired. An entry in this box is required.

3.  From the Frame Height dropdown menu, select a different height for this section, in pixels.

4.  To save your changes, click Update Options.
